HOUSE BILL No. 1087
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 12-21-5-10; IC 20-34-4.5.
Synopsis: Overdose reversal medication. Provides that, subject to
available funding, the division of mental health and addiction may
develop an application to provide funds to a school corporation or
school maintained by a school corporation to obtain a federal Food and
Drug Administration approved opioid overdose reversal medication.
Provides that the state health commissioner shall issue a statewide
standing order, prescription, or protocol for a federal Food and Drug
Administration approved opioid overdose reversal medication for each
public school or school corporation.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Andrade, Bascom, Pfaff
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Health.
