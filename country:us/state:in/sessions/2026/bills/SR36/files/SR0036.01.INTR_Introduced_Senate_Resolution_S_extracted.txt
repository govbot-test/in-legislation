, read first time and referred to Committee on

Introduced
Second Regular Session 124th General Assembly (2026)
SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring the 2026 Senate
2 Majority Caucus interns and thanking them for their faithful
3 service to the Indiana General Assembly.
4 Whereas, Throughout the Second Regular Session of the
5 One Hundred Twenty-Fourth Indiana General Assembly, the
6 Senate Majority Caucus interns have provided the Senators of
7 the Republican Caucus, individually and collectively, with
8 outstanding assistance and support;
9 Whereas, Those serving so ably this session include Paris
10 Alexander, Aaron Ambrose, Amelia Austin, Nicholas Beaver,
11 Nicholas Biehl, Skylar Bilderback, Michael Bouffard, Jose
12 Boyzo, Alexis Brock, Duncan Cherry, Erin DeBettencourt,
13 Audrey Donaldson, Everett Fehr, Will Fluhr, Cameron Fuesler,
14 Jack Gillard, Salil Gupta, David Hopper, Gurleen Kaur, Icarus
15 Kim, Paras Malhotra, Leo Martinez, Cole McNeill, Karlee
16 Monday, Donald Moore, John Olson, Aydyn Patterson, James
17 Reichmann, Ivanna Rodriguez, Kennedy Seals, Austin Thies,
18 Lindsey Webb, Samiha Zaman, and Victoria Zigenfus;
19 Whereas, The Senate Majority Caucus interns represent
20 fifteen colleges and universities, including The American
2026 RR 3528/DI LW
2
1 College of Greece, Ball State University, Bowling Green State
2 University, Butler University, Indiana University Bloomington,
3 Indiana University East, Indiana University Indianapolis,
4 Indiana University Robert H. McKinney School of Law, Indiana
5 University School of Medicine, Oklahoma State University,
6 Purdue University, Purdue University Northwest, University of
7 Indianapolis, University of Southern Indiana, and Valparaiso
8 University;
9 Whereas, The exemplary efforts of the Senate Majority
10 Caucus interns have contributed to the Senate's ability to be
11 more efficient and responsive to the citizens of the State of
12 Indiana; and
13 Whereas, It is fitting that the Senate Majority Caucus
14 interns are recognized for their hard work, time devoted, and
15 talents shared with the Indiana General Assembly: Therefore,
16 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
17 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
18 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors and expresses
19 its full appreciation and sincere commendation to the 2026
20 Senate Majority Caucus interns.
21 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
22 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Emma Balzer, Senate
23 Majority Intern Director, and each Senate Majority Caucus
24 intern who served during the Second Regular Session of the
25 One Hundred Twenty-Fourth General Assembly.
