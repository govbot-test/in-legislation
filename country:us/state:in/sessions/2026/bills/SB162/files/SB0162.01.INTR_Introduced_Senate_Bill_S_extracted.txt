DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 22-4.
Synopsis: Department of workforce development. Removes vacation
pay and sick pay from the definition of "deductible income". Amends
the definition of "suitable work" to apply solely to extended benefit
claims. Amends the definition of "employment" with respect to certain
religious, charitable, or educational organizations. Provides that the
department of workforce development may make certain direct deposit
disbursements under limited circumstances. Provides that the date a
determination of eligibility is sent is prima facie evidence of proper
service. Removes outdated or expired provisions. Removes references
to dependants' allowances for purposes of calculating extended
benefits. Provides that an individual who files a disaster unemployment
assistance claim may be eligible for additional benefits under certain
circumstances. Makes conforming changes.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Rogers
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Pensions and Labor.

SENATE BILL No. 162
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 22-4.
Synopsis: Department of workforce development. Removes vacation
pay and sick pay from the definition of "deductible income". Amends
the definition of "suitable work" to apply solely to extended benefit
claims. Amends the definition of "employment" with respect to certain
religious, charitable, or educational organizations. Provides that the
department of workforce development may make certain direct deposit
disbursements under limited circumstances. Provides that the date a
determination of eligibility is sent is prima facie evidence of proper
service. Removes outdated or expired provisions. Removes references
to dependants' allowances for purposes of calculating extended
benefits. Provides that an individual who files a disaster unemployment
assistance claim may be eligible for additional benefits under certain
circumstances. Makes conforming changes.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Rogers
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Pensions and Labor.
