DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 34-26-5.
Synopsis: Military protection order. Requires a party to a protective
order proceeding to inform the court of any military protective orders
issued by a military commander in effect between the parties. Allows
a party to file a military protective order as evidence in a protective
order proceeding and provides that the court may issue an ex parte
protective order based upon this evidence.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Carrasco
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Judiciary.
