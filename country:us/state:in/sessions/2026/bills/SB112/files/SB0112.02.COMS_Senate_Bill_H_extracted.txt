DIGEST OF SB 112 (Updated January 12, 2026 11:10 am - DI 140)
Citations Affected: IC 3-5.
Synopsis: Candidacy documents. Provides that when an individual
files a notice of withdrawal listing an address different from the address
on the individual's voter registration record, the individual's signature
on the notice of withdrawal constitutes a request to the county voter
registration office that the address on the individual's voter registration
record be the same as the address on the notice of withdrawal.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Schmitt
December 9, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Elections.
January 12, 2026, reported favorably — Do Pass.
