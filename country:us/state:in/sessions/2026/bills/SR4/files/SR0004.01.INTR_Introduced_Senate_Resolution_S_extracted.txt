MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION memorializing Timothy Faulk.
2 Whereas, Timothy (Tim) E. Faulk, former Pike Township
3 Fire Chief, professor, and mentor, passed away on December
4 28,2025, at the age of 75;
5 Whereas, Tim received his bachelor's degree from Indiana
6 University-Purdue University Indianapolis;
7 Whereas, Tim fulfilled a lifelong dream when he began his
8 career as a volunteer at the Pike Township Fire Department in
9 1974, eventually becoming one of the first six paid firefighters
10 in 1975 and retiring as Fire Chief in 2000; Whereas, Tim was
11 passionate about the safety of the people and he was
12 instrumental, along with others, in laying the groundwork for
13 Pike Township Fire Department and shaping what it has become
14 today;
15 Whereas, After retiring from the Pike Township fire
16 Department, Tim became a professor at Ivy Tech Community
17 College where he taught fire science and safety classes and
18 worked as the program chair;
