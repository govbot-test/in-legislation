HOUSE BILL No. 1049
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 5-10-8-27; IC 12-15-5-7; IC 27-8-40;
IC 27-13-7-29.
Synopsis: Coverage for doula services. Requires a state employee
health plan, policy of accident and sickness insurance, and health
maintenance organization contract to provide coverage for doula
services. Requires Medicaid pregnancy services to include
reimbursement for doula services.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Summers
December 5, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Insurance.
