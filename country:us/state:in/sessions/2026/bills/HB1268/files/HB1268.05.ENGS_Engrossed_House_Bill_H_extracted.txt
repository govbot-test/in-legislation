Citations Affected: IC 4-3; IC 5-28.
Synopsis: Indiana defense task force funding. Adds the adjutant
general of the Indiana National Guard or the adjutant general's designee
as a nonvoting member of the Indiana defense task force (task force).
Provides that, on or before May 1, 2026, and each May 1 thereafter, the
Indiana defense task force shall submit a report to the corporation's
office of defense development and the budget committee. Provides that
the report shall identify the task force's identified priorities for
expenditures for the previous state fiscal year. Provides that on or
