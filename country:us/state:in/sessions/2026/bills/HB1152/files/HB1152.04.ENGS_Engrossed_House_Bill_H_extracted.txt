DIGEST OF HB 1152 (Updated February 17, 2026 5:38 pm - DI 149)
Citations Affected: IC 32-21; IC 32-25.5.
Synopsis: Homeowners association matters. Allows, if certain
conditions are met, a homeowners association to increase a budget,
without a quorum, in an amount not to exceed 110% of the amount of
the last approved budget, within five years after the first sale of a lot or
unit from a developer to a person not associated with the developer.
Prohibits a homeowners association from charging a fee associated
with any service provided by the homeowners association. Allows, if
