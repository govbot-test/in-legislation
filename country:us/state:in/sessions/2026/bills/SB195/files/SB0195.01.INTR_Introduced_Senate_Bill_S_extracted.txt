SENATE BILL No. 195

DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 12-14-30-4.
Synopsis: Eligibility for SNAP benefits. Eliminates the countable
asset limitation for resources with respect to expanded categorical
eligibility for the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program.
Effective: July 1, 2026.

Services.
