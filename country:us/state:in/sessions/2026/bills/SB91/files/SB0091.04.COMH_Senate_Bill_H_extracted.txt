DIGEST OF SB 91 (Updated February 10, 2026 11:42 am - DI 147)
Citations Affected: IC 16-41.
Synopsis: Syringe exchange program. Amends the duties of a
qualified entity that operates a syringe exchange program (program).
Establishes requirements for program participation. Prohibits a
qualified entity that operates a program from providing chemical
reagents or precursors. Extends the expiration of the program to July
1, 2028.
Effective: Upon passage.
