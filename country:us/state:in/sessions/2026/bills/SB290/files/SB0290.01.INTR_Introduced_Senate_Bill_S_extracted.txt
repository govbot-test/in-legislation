Citations Affected: IC 10-13-3-27; IC 11-8-8; IC 11-10-11.5-11;

IC 35-41-4-2; IC 35-42-4; IC 35-44.1-3-9; IC 35-50-2-14.
Synopsis: Sex with a minor. Adds the criminal offense of sex with a
minor, which is committed when a person who is at least: (1) 24 years
of age and who engages in sexual intercourse or other sexual conduct,
fondling, or touching with a child who is 16 years of age; or (2) 25
years of age and who engages in sexual intercourse or other sexual
conduct, fondling, or touching with a child who is 16 or 17 years of
age. Makes conforming amendments.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Bohacek
January 12, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Corrections and Criminal
Law.
