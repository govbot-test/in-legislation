SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION recognizing the South Putnam
2 High School football team on winning the 2025 Indiana High
3 School Athletic Association (IHSAA) Class 1A state
4 championship title.
5 Whereas, On November 28, 2025, the South Putnam High
6 School football team finalized their season at Lucas Oil
7 Stadium in Indianapolis, Indiana, by winning their first football
8 state championship title in thirty-nine years;
9 Whereas, With a 12-2 season, the South Putnam Eagles
10 advanced to the state championship game against Pioneer High
11 School after multiple thrilling match-ups, including a shutout
12 game against Sullivan High School with a 10-0 score and a
13 landslide semi-state game against Milan High School with a
14 40-7 score;
15 Whereas, Following a balanced fight in the first half, it took
16 just one minute and thirty-five seconds for the South Putnam
17 Eagles to take a two-score lead in the third quarter,
18 capitalizing on five consecutive runs by junior running back Ty
19 Benton and a recovered fumble by junior defensive end Khalil
20 Jefferson;
2026 RR 3538/DI LW

2
1 Whereas, With twelve minutes remaining in the fourth
2 quarter, South Putnam continued its momentum with three
3 consecutive scoring possessions and thirty-six points on the
4 board, capped off by a forty-seven-yard interception run back
5 by junior quarterback Drew Cline acting as corner-back;
6 Whereas, Through grit and determination, the South
7 Putnam Eagles won the championship game with a final score
8 of 55-29; and
9 Whereas, Under the leadership of Head Coach Chuck
10 Sorrell, the Eagles finished their 2025 season with a final
11 record of 13-2 and their second state football title in school
12 history: Therefore,
13 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
14 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
15 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate congratulates the
16 South Putnam High School football team on winning the 2025
17 IHSAA 1A state championship.
18 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
19 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Head Coach Chuck
20 Sorrell, Principal Tona Gardner, and members of the South
21 Putnam High School football team.
