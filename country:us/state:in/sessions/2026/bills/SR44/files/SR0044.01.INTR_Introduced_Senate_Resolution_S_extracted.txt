Introduced
SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Senator Eric Bassler
2 upon his retirement from the Indiana Senate.
3 Whereas, Senator Eric Bassler has faithfully and honorably
4 served the constituents of Senate District 39, encompassing
5 Daviess, Greene, Knox, and Martin counties and portions of
6 Owen and Sullivan counties, since he was elected in 2014;
7 Whereas, During his tenure, Senator Bassler has served as
8 chairman of the Senate Committees on Ethics, Insurance, and
9 the Senate Subcommittee on School Funding, as well as a
10 member on the Senate Committees on Appropriations, Rules
11 and Legislative Procedure, and Environmental Affairs;
12 Whereas, Outside of being a legislator, Senator Bassler is
13 a financial advisor, having had international experience in
14 business development previously, has served as an adjunct
15 professor of economics at Indiana University Northwest, and
16 owns Scoops Homemade Ice Cream Shop and Cherry Ghost
17 coffee shop in Washington, Indiana;
18 Whereas, Senator Bassler previously served on the City of
19 Washington Common Council and the City of Washington
2026 RR 3519/DI JR
2
1 Board of Public Works and Safety, as well as serving the
2 Catholic Education Endowment Board, the Daviess County
3 Community Foundation, the Daviess County United Way,
4 Daviess County YMCA, Edward Jones Leadership Team, and
5 Purdue Extension 4-H Community Projects Board;
6 Whereas, Senator Bassler resides in Washington with his
7 wife, Julie, is the proud father of Grace, Catherine, Margaret,
8 Will, and Nick, and holds a bachelor's degree in chemistry and
9 psychology from Indiana University and a master's degree in
10 economics;
11 Whereas, Senator Bassler's career in the Indiana Senate
12 should be characterized as truly living out his faith and values
13 in the execution of his service to his constituents, as he has
14 approached every issue and vote with a servant's heart, never
15 self-serving, but rather how best he can serve;
16 Whereas, Senator Bassler is a valued member of the
17 Indiana Senate extended family, his quiet leadership but
18 joyfully lived life and faith are ever-present, and he will be
19 greatly missed: Therefore,
20 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
21 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
22 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors Senator Eric
23 Bassler upon his retirement, thanks him for his faithful service
24 to the Indiana Senate and the people of Senate District 39, and
25 wishes him and his family all the best.
26 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
27 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Senator Eric Bassler and
28 his family.
