HOUSE BILL No. 1018
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 12-17-12.
Synopsis: School age child care. Removes certain transportation
requirements in the approval criteria for the school age child care
project fund. Requires the division of family resources to adopt rules
specifying cost and expense standards for transporting a child to a
facility where the child does not attend school.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
DeVon
December 1, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Family, Children and
Human Affairs.
