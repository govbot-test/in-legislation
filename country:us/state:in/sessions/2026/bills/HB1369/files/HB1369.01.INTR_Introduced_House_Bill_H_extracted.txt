HOUSE BILL No. 1369
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 6-1.1; IC 36-7-32.5-15.
Synopsis: Various property tax matters. Expires various property tax
exemptions allowed in current law. Provides that certain property tax
abatements may not be granted after December 31, 2030. Authorizes
a county fiscal body to adopt an ordinance that exempts certain
homesteads owned by an individual who is at least 65 years of age from
property taxation. Makes corresponding changes.
Effective: July 1, 2026; January 1, 2027.
Lucas
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Ways and Means.
