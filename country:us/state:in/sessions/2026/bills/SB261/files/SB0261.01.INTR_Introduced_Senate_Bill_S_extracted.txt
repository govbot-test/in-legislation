DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 9-24-2-2.5; IC 31-9-2-29.5; IC 31-37-19-17;
IC 33-39-1-8; IC 35-31.5-2; IC 35-43-1; IC 35-45-3-2; IC 35-46-10.
Synopsis: Criminal vandalism. Renames the crime of "criminal
mischief" to "criminal vandalism". Repeals: (1) institutional criminal
mischief; (2) cemetery mischief; (3) railroad mischief; and (4) critical
infrastructure facility mischief; places them in the "criminal vandalism"
statute, and preserves the original penalties and defenses. Repeals and
places provisions involving damage to a cave in the criminal vandalism
statute. Repeals and places provisions involving depositing refuse in a
cave in the littering statute. Makes conforming amendments.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Young M
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Corrections and Criminal
Law.
