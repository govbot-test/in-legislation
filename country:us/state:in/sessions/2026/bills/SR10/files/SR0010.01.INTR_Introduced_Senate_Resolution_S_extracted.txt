Second Regular Session 124th General Assembly (2026)
SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Home Repairs for
2 Good.
3 Whereas, Home Repairs for Good (formerly NeighborLink
4 Indianapolis) is a non-profit organization, established in 2013,
5 rooted in Marion County;
6 Whereas, Home Repairs for Good provides free critical
7 home repairs to low-income seniors and individuals with
8 disabilities, allowing them to age safely and with dignity;
9 Whereas, Home Repairs for Good started with a handful of
10 volunteers that are passionate about helping and supporting the
11 community while constantly team-building;
12 Whereas, Home Repairs for Good provides drain cleaning
13 services, exterior repairs, electrical repairs, plumbing,
14 vegetation cleanup, large-scale yard work, gutter cleaning, and
15 other repairs and replacement;
16 Whereas, Home Repairs for Good also offers home
17 stabilization, client services, workforce development
18 opportunities, and growth programs to help adults and
2026 DR 3832/DI ol
2
1 individuals with disabilities;
2 Whereas, The Home Specialization Program (HSP) offered
3 by Home Repairs for Good focuses on a small subset of the
4 homes, not just for the homeowner who lives there but for their
5 families and future generations; and
6 Whereas, One of the program's objectives is to provide
7 avenues of specialty to individuals that may not otherwise have
8 the opportunity to gain those specialties and the certifications
9 to pursue them: Therefore,
10 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
11 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
12 SECTION 1. That the Indiana State Senate honors Home
13 Repairs for Good.
14 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
15 to transmit two copies of this Resolution to Home Repairs for
16 Good.
