DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 9-24-20.
Synopsis: Background colors for licenses and permits. Requires the
bureau of motor vehicles to apply a unique background color to a
driver's license, a learner's permit, or an identification card issued after
December 31, 2026, to an individual who: (1) is an alien with lawful
status; or (2) is not registered to vote at the time of issuance.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Isa, Commons, Pressel
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Roads and Transportation.

HOUSE BILL No. 1371
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
motor vehicles.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 9-24-20 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE AS
2 A NEW CHAPTER TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
3 1, 2026]:
4 Chapter 20. Unique Background Colors for Certain Licenses,
5 Permits, and Identification Cards
6 Sec. 1. This chapter applies to:
7 (1) a driver's license;
8 (2) a learner's permit; and
9 (3) an identification card;
10 issued by the bureau after December 31, 2026.
11 Sec. 2. (a) The bureau shall designate and apply a unique
12 background color to a driver's license, a learner's permit, or an
13 identification card issued to an individual who:
14 (1) is an alien with lawful status, as defined in
15 IC 9-13-2-92.3(2); or
16 (2) is not registered to vote at the time of issuance.
17 (b) All other aspects of a driver's license, a learner's permit, or

2
1 an identification card with a unique background color under
2 subsection (a) must be the same as a driver's license, a learner's
3 permit, or an identification card typically issued by the bureau.
4 Sec. 3. Not later than thirty (30) days after the bureau receives
5 a completed online voter registration under IC 3-7-26.7 from an
6 individual who was previously issued a driver's license, a learner's
7 permit, or an identification card with a unique background color
8 under section 2 of this chapter, the bureau must notify the
9 individual that the individual is eligible for a new driver's license,
10 learner's permit, or identification card.
