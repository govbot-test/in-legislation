DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 35-31.5-2; IC 35-45-4.
Synopsis: Digital sexual image abuse. Increases the penalty for the
crime of distribution of an intimate image, from a Class A
misdemeanor to a Level 6 felony, and increases the enhancement of
this crime from a Level 6 felony to a Level 5 felony. Defines "digital
sexual image" as a digital, computer generated image or video that is
created or modified to depict: (1) sexual intercourse; (2) other sexual
conduct; or (3) exhibition of certain body parts; of a quality,
characteristic, or condition, such that it appears to depict an individual.
Makes creation or possession of a digital sexual image a Class A
misdemeanor and distribution of a digital sexual image a Level 6
felony.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Haggard, Bauer M
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Courts and Criminal Code.
