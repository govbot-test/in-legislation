HOUSE BILL No. 1402
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 35-46-1-11.4.
Synopsis: Restrictions on location of tobacco and vaping shops.
Amends a provision to prohibit the operation of a tobacco and vaping
business within 3,520 feet of certain school buildings. (Current law
requires 1,000 feet.)
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Dant Chesser, Clere, Miller K
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Policy.
