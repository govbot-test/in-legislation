DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 16-41-21.1.
Synopsis: Testing drinking water for lead in school buildings.
Requires the person or entity having authority over a school building
to: (1) test the drinking water in the school building by a specified time
frame; and (2) follow certain guidelines in conducting the testing and
remediation. Requires the Indiana department of health to impose a
civil penalty on the superintendent of a school corporation or the
equivalent for a charter school for failing to have the school building's
drinking water tested within the specified time frame.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Jackson C, Aylesworth
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Health.
