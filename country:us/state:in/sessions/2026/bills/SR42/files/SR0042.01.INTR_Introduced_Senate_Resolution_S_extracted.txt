MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION to memorialize Alicia J.
2 Collins.
3 Whereas, Alicia June Barnett Collins was born on March
4 15, 1971, and passed away on October 18, 2025, at the age of
5 54;
6 Whereas, Alicia is survived by her loving family, including
7 her husband Tom Collins, son Trajan S. Collins, daughter
8 Blake A. Hall, sister Ashley Barnett, and mother Sadie Barnett;
9 Whereas, Alicia dedicated her adult life to serving the Far
10 Eastside of Indianapolis, and leaves behind a legacy of
11 philanthropy, grassroots organizing, crime prevention, and
12 community development;
13 Whereas, Over the course of her thirty-year career, Alicia
14 worked for Making Connections, an initiative of the Annie E.
15 Casey Foundation, and the Central Indiana Community
16 Foundation, where she spent fourteen years on staff and
17 became Director of Community Leadership;
18 Whereas, In March 2022, Alicia took a leap of faith,
2026 DR 3853/DI aj
2
1 becoming an independent consultant and founding her own
2 company, 3-R Consulting, LLC, guided by the principle that
3 "Relationships + Resources = Results";
4 Whereas, Outside of her work, Alicia served as an
5 Indianapolis campaign representative for President Barack
6 Obama's campaigns and for his legacy advocacy group,
7 Organizing for Action (OFA);
8 Whereas, Alicia's passion for her work and belief in public
9 service was evident in all she did, and she aspired to instill that
10 passion in her daughter Blake, often taking her to community
11 meetings and giving her a firsthand example of servant
12 leadership; and
13 Whereas, The Far Eastside and Indiana Senate District 34
14 are a better place because of Alicia's important work, and she
15 will be fondly remembered by her family, friends, colleagues,
16 and community for all her contributions to our city: Therefore,
17 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
18 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
19 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate memorializes Alicia
20 June Barnett Collins.
21 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is directed to
22 transmit copies of this Resolution to Tom Collins, Trajan S.
23 Collins, Blake Hall, Sadie Barnett, and Ashley Barnett.
