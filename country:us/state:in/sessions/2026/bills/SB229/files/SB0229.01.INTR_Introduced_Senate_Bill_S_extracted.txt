DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 32-32-3; IC 33-42-13-3.
Synopsis: Various transaction matters. Provides that certain conditions
must be met before a person may sign a sales contract to purchase an
interest in a time share unit. Provides that a purchaser has the right to
cancel a camping club membership or time share purchase within 14
days after the execution of the sales contract, excluding Sundays and
legal holidays (current law is 72 hours). Prohibits a notary public from
performing a notarial act for a transaction when the notary public is an:
(1) interest holder; or (2) officer, director, manager, or other agent; of
a party to the transaction.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Donato
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Judiciary.

28 mail or telegraph is effective at the time of delivery at the place of
29 business of the developer or escrow agent designated in the form of
30 notice of cancellation.
