SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION expressing appreciation to the
2 Indiana State Chiropractic Association for coordinating and
3 operating the "Chiropractor of the Day" program at the Indiana
4 Statehouse and to the dedicated chiropractors who participated.
5 Whereas, The Indiana State Chiropractic Association
6 provided a great service to the Indiana State Senate during the
7 Second Regular Session of the One Hundred Twenty-Fourth
8 Indiana General Assembly by operating the "Chiropractor of
9 the Day" program at the Indiana Statehouse;
10 Whereas, For the program, the Indiana State Chiropractic
11 Association provided competent and experienced chiropractors
12 who donated their time and expertise to provide care to the
13 Senators and staff during the busy legislative session; and
14 Whereas, Each member of the Indiana State Senate is
15 appreciative of the fine efforts of the dedicated chiropractors
16 who traveled to the Statehouse to contribute their services to
17 the program: Therefore,
18 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
19 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:

2
1 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate expresses its sincere
2 appreciation to the Indiana State Chiropractic Association for
3 coordinating and operating the "Chiropractor of the Day"
4 program and to the dedicated chiropractors who participated.
5 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
6 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Dr. Derek Dyer,
7 President of the Indiana State Chiropractic Association, and
8 each chiropractor who participated in the "Chiropractor of the
9 Day" program.
