SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Senator Andy Zay
2 upon his retirement from the Indiana Senate.
3 Whereas, Senator Andy Zay has faithfully and honorably
4 served the constituents of Senate District 17, encompassing
5 Grant, Huntington, and Wabash counties, since being elected
6 in 2016;
7 Whereas, During his tenure, Senator Zay served in a
8 variety of roles, including on the Senate Committees on
9 Commerce and Technology, Pensions and Labor, and Utilities,
10 as ranking member of the Senate Committee on Environmental
11 Affairs, and as Chair of the Senate Committee on Insurance
12 and Financial Insitutions;
13 Whereas, Hailing from Huntington, Indiana, Senator Zay
14 prided himself on focusing on the needs of rural Hoosier
15 communities;
16 Whereas, In a major achievement while he was in office,
17 Senator Zay authored Senate Enrolled Act 365 in 2019, which
18 established the CarePortal in Indiana to connect the
19 Department of Child Services and faith-based community

1 programs to strengthen child welfare resources in local

3 Whereas, Senator Zay also authored Senate Enrolled Act

5 Rural Affairs to establish the Indiana Broadband Connectivity
6 Portal for Hoosiers to request broadband service;
7 Whereas, Outside of being a legislator, Senator Zay owns
8 and operates Zay Leasing and Rentals, holds both an
9 undergraduate and graduate degree from Indiana University's
10 School of Public and Environmental Affairs, and he and his
11 wife, Cindy, have three sons, two daughters, and four
12 grandchildren; and
13 Whereas, Senator Zay is a valued member of the Indiana
14 Senate extended family and his passion, dedication, and
15 devotion will be greatly missed, even as he continues to serve
16 our state on the Indiana Utility Regulatory Commission:
17 Therefore,
18 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
19 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
20 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors Senator Andy
21 Zay upon his retirement, thanks him for his faithful service to
22 the Indiana Senate and the people of Senate District 17, and
23 wishes him all the best as he serves on the Indiana Utility
24 Regulatory Commission.
25 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
26 to transmit a copy of this Resolution to Senator Andy Zay and
27 his family.
