SENATE BILL No. 170
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 36-1-12-4.
Synopsis: Local public work project bidding. Provides additional
considerations that a local public works board may consider in
determining the lowest responsible and responsive bidder for a public
work project costing at least $300,000.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Dernulc
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Pensions and Labor.
