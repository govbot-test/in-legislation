Introduced
SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Greg Gordon upon
2 his retirement from the Indiana Senate.
3 Whereas, Greg Gordon began his career with the Indiana
4 Senate in 1988 in the Senate duplicating office after proudly
5 graduating from Indiana University;
6 Whereas, Throughout his career, Greg has striven to
7 provide the best possible service within his office, always
8 ensuring that assistance is answered in an expedient manner,
9 and hand-delivering items within minutes of a request;
10 Whereas, Greg has remained a steady presence through
11 major Senate renovations and office moves, including getting
12 rid of the milk machine area, updating the 3A kitchen, moving
13 communications staff around the statehouse, and many other
14 moves;
15 Whereas, Outside of his time with the Senate, Greg spends
16 his time with his family, including his wife, Sue, and adult
17 children, Patrick and Emily, and plans to travel, continue
18 helping with Special Olympics, and spend time at Lake Winona;
2026 RR 3532/DI JR
2
1 Whereas, Always on the move, Greg played tennis for years
2 and recently picked up competitive pickleball, and he also has
3 a green thumb and consistently works in his incredible outdoor
4 space and garden;
5 Whereas, Greg is the longest serving full-time staff member
6 of the Indiana Senate, where he has been an employee longer
7 than any current Senator has been in their current office,
8 served through 39 regular sessions during the tenures of eight
9 governors and nine lieutenant governors, supported three
10 president pro tempores, and witnessed 8,230 bills become laws;
11 and
12 Whereas, Greg will be deeply missed by Senators, staff, and
13 all who have come to know him through his years of service to
14 the Senate: Therefore,
15 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
16 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
17 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors Greg Gordon
18 upon his retirement from the Indiana Senate after 38 years of
19 public service.
20 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
21 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Greg Gordon.
