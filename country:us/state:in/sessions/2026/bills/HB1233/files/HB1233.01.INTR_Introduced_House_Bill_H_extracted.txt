DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 35-31.5-2; IC 35-45-1.
Synopsis: Masking offenses. Makes wearing a mask at a public
assembly a Class C misdemeanor, and increases the penalty to a Class
A misdemeanor for a second or subsequent offense. Increases the
penalty for rioting and disorderly conduct to a Level 6 felony if the
offense is committed while wearing a mask.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Yocum, Haggard
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Courts and Criminal Code.

13 person's nose or mouth. However, the term does not include the
14 following:
15 (1) A holiday costume, if worn to celebrate the holiday.
16 (2) A theater costume worn by a person participating in a
17 theater production.
