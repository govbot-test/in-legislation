SENATE BILL No. 113
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 3-10-1-31.1.
Synopsis: Preservation of election material. Extends the period within
which certain election material must be preserved from 22 months to
60 months.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Schmitt
December 9, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Elections.
