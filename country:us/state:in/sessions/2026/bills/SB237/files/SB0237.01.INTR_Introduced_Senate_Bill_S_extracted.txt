SENATE BILL No. 237
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 13-11-2; IC 13-14-2.
Synopsis: PFAS chemicals. Defines "decision", "PFAS chemicals",
and "state prioritized PFAS chemicals". Prohibits the department of
environmental management (department) from basing a decision
primarily on federal risk values that have not been promulgated
through federal rulemaking. Instructs the department to avoid the use
of federal risk values that are at or below background concentrations
in air, water, soil, or sediment. Provides that the department shall focus
on state prioritized PFAS chemicals when PFAS chemicals are relevant
to carrying out the department's duties.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Baldwin
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Environmental Affairs.
