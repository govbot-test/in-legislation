SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Samuelson Insurance
2 Agency.
3 Whereas, Samuelson Insurance Agency was founded in
4 1926 by William Samuelson, who passed away in 1953;
5 Whereas, After William's death, the business was run first
6 by William's wife, Esther Samuelson, and daughter, Evelyn
7 Hamstrom, then by William’s son-in-law, Charles Lorenz, and
8 eventually by Charles' son, Kenneth Lorenz;
9 Whereas, The agency is now run by James Hazzard, who
10 joined in 2010, assumed day-to-day leadership a few years
11 later, and fully acquired the agency from Charles Lorenz in
12 2019;
13 Whereas, The business operated out of the family home in
14 Portage until 1978, when the current Portage office was built
15 and what began as a small family operation has grown into a
16 multi-location independent agency serving Northwest Indiana
17 for a full century;
