DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 6-2.5-5-59.
Synopsis: Sales tax exemption for menstrual discharge collection

collection devices.

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 6-2.5-5-59 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
2 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
3 1, 2026]: Sec. 59. (a) For purposes of this section, "menstrual
4 discharge collection devices" means:
5 (1) tampons;
6 (2) panty liners;
7 (3) menstrual cups;
8 (4) sanitary napkins; and
9 (5) other similar tangible personal property designed to
10 promote hygiene in connection with the human menstrual
11 cycle.
12 (b) Sales of menstrual discharge collection devices are exempt
13 from the state gross retail tax.
14 SECTION 2. [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026] (a) IC 6-2.5-5-59, as
15 added by this act, applies only to retail transactions occurring after
16 June 30, 2026.
17 (b) Except as provided in subsection (c), a retail transaction is
