DIGEST OF INTRODUCED RESOLUTION
Indiana.
Synopsis: Impeachment of a judge or prosecutor. Provides that circuit
court judges and prosecuting attorneys may be impeached for crime,
incapacity, or negligence, or impeached or removed from office as
prescribed by law. This proposed amendment has not been previously
agreed to by a general assembly.
Effective: This proposed amendment must be agreed to by two
consecutive general assemblies and ratified by a majority of the state's
voters voting on the question to be effective.

HOUSE JOINT
RESOLUTION No. 1
A JOINT RESOLUTION proposing an amendment to Article 6 of
the Constitution of the State of Indiana concerning judicial officers.
Be it resolved by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. The following amendment to the Constitution of the
2 State of Indiana is proposed and agreed to by this, the One Hundred
3 Twenty-Fourth General Assembly of the State of Indiana, and is
4 referred to the next General Assembly for reconsideration and
5 agreement.
6 SECTION 2. ARTICLE 6, SECTION 7 OF THE CONSTITUTION
7 OF THE STATE OF INDIANA IS AMENDED TO READ AS
8 FOLLOWS: Section 7. All State officers, Circuit Court Judges, and
9 Prosecuting Attorneys shall, for crime, incapacity, or negligence, be
10 liable to be removed from office, either by impeachment by the House
11 of Representatives, to be tried by the Senate, or by a joint resolution of
12 the General Assembly; two-thirds of the members elected to each
13 branch voting, in either case, therefor.

2
1 SECTION 3. ARTICLE 6, SECTION 8 OF THE CONSTITUTION
2 OF THE STATE OF INDIANA IS AMENDED TO READ AS
3 FOLLOWS: Section 8. All State, county, township, and town officers,
4 and all Circuit Court Judges and Prosecuting Attorneys, may be
5 impeached, or removed from office, in such manner as may be
6 prescribed by law.
