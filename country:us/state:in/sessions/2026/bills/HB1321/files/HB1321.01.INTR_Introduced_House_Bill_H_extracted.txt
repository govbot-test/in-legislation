HOUSE BILL No. 1321
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 16-18-2; IC 16-42-5.5; IC 35-52-16-86.5.
Synopsis: Prohibition on use of certain food additives. Prohibits the
use of various food additives in Indiana.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Hatcher
January 6, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Health.

6 Chapter 5.5. Restrictions on Food Additives
7 Sec. 1. As used in this chapter, "food" has the meaning set forth

9 Sec. 2. As used in this chapter, "person" has the meaning set
10 forth in IC 16-18-2-274(a).
11 Sec. 3. As used in this chapter, "prohibited food additive" refers
12 to the following substances:
13 (1) Blue 1 (CAS 3844-45-9).
14 (2) Blue 2 (CAS 860-22-0).
15 (3) Green 3 (CAS 2353-45-9).
16 (4) Potassium bromate (CAS 7758-01-2).
17 (5) Red 3 (CAS 16423-68-0).
18 (6) Red 40 (CAS 25956-17-6).
19 (7) Yellow 5 (CAS 1934-21-0).
20 (8) Yellow 6 (CAS 2783-94-0).
21 Sec. 4. A person may not do the following in Indiana:
22 (1) Sell food containing a prohibited food additive.
23 (2) Store food containing a prohibited food additive.
24 (3) Distribute food containing a prohibited food additive.
25 (4) Use a prohibited food additive in the preparation of food.
26 (5) Manufacture a prohibited food additive.
27 Sec. 5. The state department may adopt rules under IC 4-22-2
28 to administer this chapter.
29 Sec. 6. The state health commissioner shall enforce the
30 provisions of this chapter.
31 Sec. 7. A person who recklessly violates this chapter commits a
32 Class B misdemeanor.
