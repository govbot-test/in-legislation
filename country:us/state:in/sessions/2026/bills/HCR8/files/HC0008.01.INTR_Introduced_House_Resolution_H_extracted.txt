RESOLUTION No. ____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION honoring the
2 longstanding partnership between the Province of Québec and
3 the State of Indiana.
4 Whereas, The year 2025 marks the 50th anniversary of the
5 National Conference of State Legislatures (NCSL) and the 20th
6 anniversary of Québec's inclusion as an international affiliate
7 member of the NCSL;
8 Whereas, In the past decade, Québec has strengthened its
9 interparliamentary relationships with the United States through
10 its involvement in the Conference of the Great Lakes and St.
11 Lawrence Governors and Premiers, and the Great Lakes–St.
12 Lawrence Legislative Caucus;
13 Whereas, Québec maintains close ties with the State of
14 Indiana, notably through its mutual involvement in these
15 forums;
16 Whereas, Québec and Indiana, along with the other states,
17 provinces, and cities participating in the Great Lakes–St.
18 Lawrence Conference, the Great Lakes–St. Lawrence
19 Legislative Caucus, and the Great Lakes and St. Lawrence
20 Cities Initiatives, place significant importance on issues related
21 to water, trade, and the environment;
22 Whereas, The Great Lakes and St. Lawrence states and
23 provinces manage together 20 percent of the world's surface
24 fresh water and provide drinking water for over 40 million
25 people, underscoring the importance of their relationship and
26 their strategic position in freshwater management and
2026 HC 1007/DI 140
2
1 sustainability;
2 Whereas, The Great Lakes and St. Lawrence states and
3 provinces constitute an economic region equivalent to the
4 world's third-largest economy;
5 Whereas, Québec and Indiana both wish to increase and
6 promote sustainable tourism in the Great Lakes–St. Lawrence
7 region;
8 Whereas, Québec is Indiana's second-largest trade partner
9 among Canadian provinces;
10 Whereas, Over the past five years, trade between Québec and
11 Indiana has steadily increased;
12 Whereas, The enduring partnership between Québec and
13 Indiana has spanned over a decade with the objective of
14 increasing maritime traffic between their ports. This
15 collaboration is designed to foster continued growth and
16 prosperity in the Great Lakes region through maritime trade;
17 Whereas, Québec was the United States' principal partner in
18 primary aluminum in 2024;
19 Whereas, Québec is the third-largest international buyer of
20 Indiana's motor vehicles and the third-largest international
21 buyer for motor cars and parts of motor vehicles. Québec is
22 also Indiana's top international provider of paper,
23 paperboards, and articles made from these materials;
24 Whereas, Around 15 Québec-owned companies currently
25 operate in Indiana, directly creating local jobs, while more
26 than 20 Indiana-based businesses maintain operations in
27 Québec; and
28 Whereas, This remarkable partnership should be recognized
29 appropriately on this special occasion: Therefore,
30 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives
31 of the General Assembly of the State of Indiana,
32 the Senate concurring:
33 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly honors the
