HOUSE BILL No. 1391
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 9-21-8-60; IC 35-52-9.1-20.2.
Synopsis: Use of telecommunications device while driving. Provides
that a person who knowingly or intentionally violates the statute
prohibiting use of a telecommunications device while operating a
moving motor vehicle commits a Level 5 felony if the violation is the
proximate cause of serious bodily injury to another person.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Jeter
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Courts and Criminal Code.
