I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Portage Township
2 Schools and Stratelign.
3 Whereas, Portage Township Schools have partnered with
4 Stratelign to establish a major groundbreaking innovative
5 energy saving project;
6 Whereas, The project will be implemented this year and
7 will reduce utility costs, decrease energy consumption, and
8 promote the sustainability of the school system throughout the
9 district;
10 Whereas, Stratelign collaborated with Portage Township
11 Schools to manage a comprehensive feasibility which included
12 a competitive Request for Proposal (RFP) process which was
13 tailored to identify key opportunities for improvement and
14 impactful solutions;
15 Whereas, The district is projecting to save more than $9.6
16 million through energy monitoring, LED lighting, and solar
17 technology at Portage Township Schools and these savings
18 would positively impact the district's operations fund;
