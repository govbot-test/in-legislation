SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION congratulating the Brownsburg
2 High School football team on winning the 2025 Indiana High
3 School Athletic Association (IHSAA) Class 6A state
4 championship title.
5 Whereas, On November 29, 2025, the Brownsburg High
6 School football team finalized their season at Lucas Oil
7 Stadium in Indianapolis, Indiana, for the 6A football
8 championship title as the defending 6A state champions;
9 Whereas, With a perfect 13-0 season, the Brownsburg
10 Bulldogs, ranked first overall in Class 6A, advanced to the state
11 championship game against Westfield High School after many
12 thrilling match-ups, including shutout games against Cathedral
13 High School with a 29-0 score and against Warren Central
14 High School at Semi-State with a 31-0 score;
15 Whereas, The Bulldogs controlled the game early on,
16 scoring two touchdowns in the first quarter and a 99-yard
17 kickoff return touchdown by junior Branden Sharpe in the
18 second quarter, the longest return in IHSAA state championship
19 game history;

2
1 Whereas, Despite the Westfield Shamrocks fighting back
2 with two fourth quarter touchdowns and an onside kick that
3 could have given Westfield a shot at tying or winning the game
4 with 14 seconds left, the high bouncing kick was recovered by
5 the Bulldogs, solidifying final control of the ball and the game;
6 Whereas, Through continued hard work and determination,
7 the Brownsburg Bulldogs won their second consecutive state
8 championship with a final score of 38-31;
9 Whereas, In addition to his record for the longest kickoff
10 return, Branden Sharpe also set a new state championship
11 game record for the most receiving yards with 151 yards; and
12 Whereas, Under the leadership of Head Coach John Hart,
13 the Bulldogs finished their 2025 season with a perfect record
14 and their fourth state football title in school history: Therefore,
15 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
16 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
17 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate congratulates the
18 Brownsburg High School football team on winning the 2025
19 IHSAA 6A state championship.
20 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
21 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Head Coach John Hart
22 and Principal Dr. Thomas Balitewicz.
