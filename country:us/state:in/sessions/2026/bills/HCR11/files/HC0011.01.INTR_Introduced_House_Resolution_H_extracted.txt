RESOLUTION No. ____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION honoring the Hosea
2 Family on the occasion of the 31st Biennial Hosea Family
3 Reunion.
4 Whereas, The lineage of the Hosea family is deeply rooted
5 in the history of Alabama, tracing back to their earliest known
6 ancestor, William "Bill" Hosea, born in Marengo County in
7 1815, whose legacy began in the communities of Marengo,
8 Tuscaloosa, and Jefferson Counties, and Dixon Mills;
9 Whereas, Demonstrating immense courage and resilience, the
10 ancestors of the Hosea Family participated in the Great
11 Migration, journeying from the rural South to the urban
12 Midwest to escape the oppression of the Jim Crow era and
13 build new futures, evolving from skilled farmers and
14 sharecroppers into leaders in industry, the military, law,
15 medicine, and beyond;
16 Whereas, The foundation of the biennial Hosea Family
17 Reunion was laid in the mid-1960s in Gary, Indiana, through
18 the love and vision of their matriarch, Lealer White-Hosea, and
19 her ten daughters: Bernice Hosea-Carter, Phyllis
20 Hosea-Thomas, Florence Hosea-Logan, Mary J.
21 Hosea-Thomas, Minnie L. Hosea-Thomas, Hettie M.
22 Hosea-Carter, Idella Hosea-Smith, and Beatrice
23 Hosea-McDaniel;
24 Whereas, The Hosea Family is distinctively honored by the
25 presence and continued wisdom of their surviving elders, Mary
26 A. Hosea-Comer and Marva J. Hosea-Hearn, who helped turn
27 unplanned family gatherings into the legendary reunions they

1 celebrate today;
2 Whereas, Despite the separation caused by time and
3 geography, the spirit of the Hosea family prevailed, leading to
4 a joyous reconnection in 2022 with relatives from Bloomington,
5 Illinois, who descended from Tuscaloosa, thereby restoring lost
6 branches to the family tree;
7 Whereas, The reunion of these two branches culminated in
8 the formation of the Hosea Family Association Inc., a nonprofit
9 organization certified by the State of Indiana, dedicated to
10 preserving the legacy of the Hosea family, promoting historical
11 lines, and providing mentoring and youth scholarships; and
12 Whereas, The Hosea family will host its 31st family reunion
13 in Indianapolis, Indiana, to remember their ancestors, honor
14 their elders, and continue creating stronger family bonds:
15 Therefore,
16 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives
17 of the General Assembly of the State of Indiana,
18 the Senate concurring:
19 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly honors the
20 Hosea Family on the occasion of the 31st Biennial Hosea
21 Family Reunion.
22 SECTION 2. That the Principal Clerk of the House of
23 Representatives shall transmit copies of this resolution to State
