DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 21-12.
Synopsis: Twenty-first century scholarship eligibility. Provides that
the commission for higher education may allow a student who does not
meet full-time enrollment or credit hour requirements under the
twenty-first century scholars program to be eligible or to maintain
eligibility for a twenty-first century scholarship award if the student is
a student with a disability and meets certain other requirements.
Provides that a scholarship may be renewed for the student for the
number of academic terms that constitute eight undergraduate
academic years. Makes conforming changes.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Moseley
December 5, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Education.
