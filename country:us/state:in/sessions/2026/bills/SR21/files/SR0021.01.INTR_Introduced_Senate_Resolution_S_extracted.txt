SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION celebrating the 250th
2 anniversary of the signing of the Declaration of Independence.
3 Whereas, In 2026, the United States commemorates 250
4 years since the signing of the Declaration of Independence;
5 Whereas, The 1976 bicentennial focused on celebrating the
6 nation's founding, while this semiquincentennial will look at the
7 bigger picture of the American experience and explore
8 Indiana's role in the nation's history, from the actions leading
9 to the revolutionary period until today;
10 Whereas, Known as the original western frontier, Indiana
11 has always been a crossroads of America, providing influential
12 leaders, movements, entertainers, and culture to the United
13 States at large;
14 Whereas, As we celebrate the 250th year of American
15 independence, we pay tribute to the heroes who displayed
16 courage and resilience to secure our liberty;
17 Whereas, Life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness are the
18 principal foundations of our American independence; and
2026 RR 3507/DI JR

2
1 Whereas, As Hoosiers, our own State Constitution begins
2 with "To the end that justice be established, public order
3 maintained, and liberty perpetuated," and it is a historic time
4 to honor and reflect on our state's role in our great nation's
5 past: Therefore,
6 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
7 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
8 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate celebrates the 250th
9 anniversary of the signing of the Declaration of Independence.
10 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
11 to transmit copies of this Resolution to America250 and the
12 Indiana Semiquincentennial Commission.
