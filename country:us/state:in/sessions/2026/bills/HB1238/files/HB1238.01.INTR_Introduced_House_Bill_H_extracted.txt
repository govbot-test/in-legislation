DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 6-1.1-4.
Synopsis: Assessment of homesteads. Provides that, beginning with
the January 1, 2027, assessment date, the assessed value of real
property that qualifies as a homestead shall only be adjusted upon the
sale, devise, descent, or conveyance of the real property. Provides that
the assessed value of such real property may not be adjusted before the
sale, devise, descent, or conveyance of the real property unless
requested by the owner of the real property.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Dvorak
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Ways and Means.
