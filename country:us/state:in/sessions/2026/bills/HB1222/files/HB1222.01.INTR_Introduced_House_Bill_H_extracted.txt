DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 14-8-2-243.5; IC 14-27-7.5.
Synopsis: Regulation of dams. Exempts retention ponds from dam
regulations.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Lopez
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Natural Resources.

HOUSE BILL No. 1222
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
natural and cultural resources.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 14-8-2-243.5 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
2 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 243.5. "Retention pond", for
4 purposes of IC 14-27-7.5, has the meaning set forth in
5 IC 14-27-7.5-4.5.
6 SECTION 2. IC 14-27-7.5-1, AS AMENDED BY P.L.129-2022,
7 SECTION 2, IS AMENDED TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE
8 JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 1. (a) This chapter does not apply to the following:
9 (1) A structure that meets the following conditions:
10 (A) Is built for the sole purpose of erosion control, watering
11 livestock, recreation, or providing a haven or refuge for fish or
12 wildlife.
13 (B) Has a drainage area above the dam of not more than one
14 (1) square mile.
15 (C) Does not exceed twenty (20) feet in height.
16 (D) Does not impound a volume of more than one hundred
17 (100) acre-feet of water.
2026 IN 1222—LS 6666/DI 150
2
1 (2) A structure that is regulated under the federal Mine Safety and
2 Health Act of 1977, unless the structure is proposed to be retained
3 as a permanent structure after bond release.
4 (3) Except for a structure under subsection (b), a structure that is
5 a low hazard dam or a significant hazard dam that meets only one
6 (1) of the following conditions:
7 (A) Has a drainage area above the dam of not more than one
8 (1) square mile.
9 (B) Does not exceed twenty (20) feet in height.
10 (C) Does not impound a volume of more than one hundred
11 (100) acre-feet of water.
12 (4) A retention pond.
13 (b) This chapter applies to a structure that is listed on the roster of
14 low head dams provided in IC 14-27-7.3.
15 SECTION 3. IC 14-27-7.5-4.5 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
16 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
17 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 4.5. As used in this chapter,
18 "retention pond" means a manmade basin that is:
19 (1) designed to collect and store surface water in an
20 impervious membrane liner; and
21 (2) not connected to a natural:
22 (A) river;
23 (B) stream; or
24 (C) lake.
