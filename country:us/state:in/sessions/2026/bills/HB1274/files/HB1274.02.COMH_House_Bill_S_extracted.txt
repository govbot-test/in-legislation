A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 27-2-30 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE AS
2 A NEW CHAPTER TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
3 1, 2026]:
4 Chapter 30. Gun Free Zone Notice
5 Sec. 1. This chapter applies to a property and casualty insurance
6 policy that is issued, delivered, entered into, renewed, or amended
7 after June 30, 2026.
8 Sec. 2. As used in this chapter, "gun free zone notice" means a
9 written notice stating that firearms or ammunition are prohibited
10 or otherwise restricted.
11 Sec. 3. As used in this chapter, "insured" means an individual
12 who is entitled to coverage under a property and casualty
13 insurance policy.
14 Sec. 4. As used in this chapter, "insurer" means an insurance
15 company that issues or delivers a property and casualty insurance
16 policy.
17 Sec. 5. As used in this chapter, "property and casualty
