SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Jacob W. "Jake"
2 Oakman for his distinguished service to Indiana's tourism,
3 sports, and cultural institutions.
4 Whereas, Jacob W. Oakman has devoted more than two
5 decades to advancing Indiana's tourism, sports, and cultural
6 assets through leadership roles in state government, nonprofit
7 institutions, and destination marketing organizations;
8 Whereas, Oakman currently serves as Senior Vice
9 President of External Relations for the Indianapolis Zoo, where
10 he leads public relations, marketing, government affairs,
11 institutional advancement, and community engagement efforts,
12 and where he oversaw the successful completion of a $53
13 million capital campaign, the largest in the Zoo's history;
14 Whereas, From 2022 to 2025, Oakman served as Executive
15 Director of the White River State Park Development
16 Commission, leading a 250-acre cultural attraction that
17 welcomes more than four million visitors annually, and where
18 he began a $65 million park expansion, secured a $30 million
19 grant from Lilly Endowment, Inc., and developed and executed
20 a comprehensive deferred maintenance and infrastructure

2
1 improvement program ultimately leading the park to record
2 visitation and revenue;
3 Whereas, Oakman's career reflects a unique integration of
4 tourism promotion, sports marketing, and public service,
5 including early professional experience with the Indianapolis
6 Indians baseball organization, where he was a top-performing
7 ticket sales executive, and later service with the Indiana Office
8 of Tourism Development, where he helped shape statewide
9 branding, national media outreach, and the Indiana
10 Bicentennial Torch Relay;
11 Whereas, Oakman served the State of Indiana at the highest
12 levels of executive leadership, including as Deputy Press
13 Secretary to Governor Mitch Daniels and as Director of
14 Communications, Speechwriter, and senior advisor to
15 Governor Eric Holcomb, contributing to domestic and
16 international engagement efforts and to gubernatorial
17 administrations marked by economic growth and global
18 outreach;
19 Whereas, Oakman's public service has been complemented
20 by extensive civic and nonprofit leadership, including service
21 on the boards or commissions of Visit Indy, Downtown Indy,
22 Inc., the Indiana Arts Commission, the Indiana University
23 Athletics Committee, and the National Institute for Fitness and
24 Sport, strengthening Indiana's cultural, athletic, and downtown
25 development ecosystems;
26 Whereas, A proud graduate of Indiana University, Oakman
27 earned a Bachelor of Science in Secondary Education and was
28 elected student body president his senior year, then continued
29 his education with a Master of Business Administration from
30 the University of Southern Indiana, reflecting a lifelong
31 commitment to leadership, public engagement, and Hoosier
32 institutions; and
33 Whereas, In recognition of his exemplary service, Oakman

6 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors Jacob W.

9 life, and commends him for strengthening the state's reputation
10 as a destination for visitors, residents, and businesses alike.
11 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
12 to transmit a copy of this Resolution to Jacob W. Oakman.
