HOUSE BILL No. 1390
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 32-24-1.
Synopsis: Eminent domain. Requires the Indiana department of
transportation (department) to make an original offer that is equal to
125% of the fair market value of a property when purchasing the
property for a project. Provides that a person having an interest in a
property that is damaged as a result of a public use is entitled to bring
an inverse condemnation claim.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Lindauer
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Roads and Transportation.

1 SECTION 1. IC 32-24-1-5.1 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
2 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
3 1, 2026]: Sec. 5.1. (a) This section applies to an offer to purchase
4 property under section 5 of this chapter that is made by the
5 Indiana department of transportation after June 30, 2026.
6 (b) The Indiana department of transportation must provide an
7 original offer to an owner that is equal to one hundred twenty-five
8 percent (125%) of the fair market value of the property, as

10 chapter.

14 or damaged as a result of a public use without the procedures of this
15 article or any prior law followed is entitled to have the person's
16 damages assessed under this article substantially in the manner
17 provided in this article.
