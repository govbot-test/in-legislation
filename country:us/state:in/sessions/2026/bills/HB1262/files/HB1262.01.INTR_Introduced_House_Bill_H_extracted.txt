HOUSE BILL No. 1262
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 20-19-3-39.
Synopsis: Report on civics education. Requires, not later than October
1, 2026, the department of education to: (1) prepare a report that
includes information and recommendations, as applicable, regarding
certain civics curriculum, initiatives, and assessments and certain
longitudinal methodologies; and (2) submit the report to the general
assembly. Establishes requirements regarding the preparation of the
report.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Jeter
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Education.
