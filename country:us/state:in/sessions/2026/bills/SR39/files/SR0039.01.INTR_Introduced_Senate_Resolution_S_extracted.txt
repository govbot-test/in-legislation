SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION recognizing Fernando
2 Mendoza for his accomplishments on and off the football field.
3 Whereas, Fernando Mendoza was born October 1, 2003, to
4 Fernando Mendoza, Sr, and Elsa Mendoza;
5 Whereas, Fernando attended Christopher Columbus High
6 School in Miami, Florida, where he lettered in tennis, was the
7 campus ministry president, school vice president, social media
8 director for the honors program, helped the Explorers win their
9 first-ever Florida High School Athletic Association Class 8A
10 state championship with a final score of 21 to 20, and
11 completed his high school career with 224 of 332 passing
12 completions for 2,222 yards and 29 touchdowns;
13 Whereas, Tabbed as the number 72 quarterback nationally
14 by ESPN, Fernando initially committed to Yale University in
15 2021 before decommitting in 2022 to play for the University of
16 California, Berkeley, also known as Cal;
17 Whereas, As a redshirt freshman at Cal in 2023, Fernando
18 appeared in nine games with eight starts, was an honorable
19 mention for Pac-12 Freshman Offensive Player of the Year,

2
1 was an All-Pac-12 team pick by Phil Steele, earned Pac-12 Fall
2 Academic Honor Roll, and was named Cal's Jonathan and Judy
3 Hoff Football Scholar-Athlete of the Year;
4 Whereas, During his sophomore season, Fernando started
5 the first 11 games of the season and was a three-time ACC
6 Quarterback of the Week after producing the ninth 3,000-yard
7 passing season in Cal history and finishing top-10 on Cal's
8 single-season passing charts in total offensive yards, passing
9 efficiency, and passing yards with 3,109 yards, 144.59, and
10 3,004 yards, respectively;
11 Whereas, Fernando earned his bachelor's degree in
12 business administration from Berkeley's Haas School of
13 Business in 2025 after only three years and completed
14 internships in the summer of 2023 and 2024 at two real estate
15 investment firms while still managing early-morning football
16 training;
17 Whereas, Prior to the 2024 LA Bowl, Fernando entered
18 into the transfer portal, where he was recruited to Indiana
19 University;
20 Whereas, While at IU, Fernando began pursuing his
21 Master's degree in Business Administration and Management
22 at the Kelley School of Business while taking on a full-time role
23 as IU's top quarterback;
24 Whereas, During the 2025 season, Fernando led the
25 Football Bowl Division in passing touchdowns, with 41, led the
26 Power 4 in touchdowns accounted for with 47, all while
27 completing 273 of 379 passing attempts with a 72 percent
28 completion percentage, 3,535 passing yards, six interceptions,
29 276 rushing yards, seven rushing touchdowns, and a passer
30 rating of 182.9;
31 Whereas, While at IU, Fernando racked up numerous
32 awards, including the Maxwell Award and Walter Camp

3
1 National Player of the Year, Davey O'Brien Award, the
2 Manning Award, the Johnny Unitas Golden Arm Award Top 5,
3 named first-team All-American from the Associated Press,
4 American Football Coaches Association, Football Writers
5 Association of American and Walter Camp Football
6 Foundation, first-team All-Big Ten, and earned the
7 Grange-Griffin Big Ten Championship Game MVP,
8 Graham-George Offensive Player of the Year, and
9 Griese-Brees Quarterback of the Year;
10 Whereas, Culminating an incredible year, Fernando was
11 the first-ever Cuban-American and first-ever Indiana finalist
12 and winner of the Heisman Trophy, awarded annually to the
13 most outstanding NCAA college football player, after receiving
14 643 first choice votes, 191 second place votes, and 51 third
15 place votes, being included in 95.16 percent of the total ballots,
16 tied for the second-highest percentage in history;
17 Whereas, Outside of his historic football achievements,
18 Fernando has been consistently supporting local communities
19 and those in need, including coordinating a turkey drive for
20 families in need in Miami-Dade County, being a founding
21 member of They Can Too, a global organization dedicated to
22 helping and spreading awareness for youth in need, organizing
23 a Cuban relief service trip, and being one of three hosts of the
24 2024 Golden Buddies Football Clinic for youth with and
25 without disabilities;
26 Whereas, Fernando has elected to enter into the 2026
27 National Football League Draft, and will have an opportunity
28 to continue his football career and expand on his years of
29 dedication to community service; and
30 Whereas, Although not a Hoosier by birth, Fernando has
31 quickly demonstrated qualities of a true Hoosier through his
32 grit, determination, selflessness, honor, and community
33 involvement: Therefore,

1 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
2 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
3 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate recognizes
4 Fernando Mendoza for his accomplishments on and off the
5 football field and wishes him well as he continues on in the
6 National Football League.
7 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby
8 directed to transmit copies of this Resolution to Fernando
9 Mendoza and his family.
