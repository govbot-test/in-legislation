DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 31-25-2-11.1.
Synopsis: Children in need of services. Requires the department of
child services (department) to conduct an in-person assessment: (1)
before closing an investigation involving a child who the department
has reason to believe is a child in need of services (CHINS); and (2)
before recommending discharge of the child who is the subject of an
open CHINS case with the juvenile court.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Garcia Wilburn, Burton
December 1, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Judiciary.

HOUSE BILL No. 1036
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
family law and juvenile law.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 31-25-2-11.1 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
2 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 11.1. (a) Before the department
4 closes an investigation involving a child who the department has
5 reason to believe is a child in need of services, the department shall
6 conduct an in-person assessment with the child not more than five
7 (5) business days before closing the investigation.
8 (b) Before the department recommends discharging a child who
9 is the subject of an open child in need of services case pending
10 before a juvenile court, the department shall conduct an in-person
11 assessment with the child not more than five (5) business days
12 before recommending discharge of the child.
