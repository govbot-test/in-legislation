SENATE RESOLUTION 20
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION urging the Indiana Senate to
2 create a Mental Health Task Force.
3 Whereas, The Task Force will issue a report to the general
4 assembly and the governor;
5 Whereas, Factors at the individual, family, community, and
6 societal levels can influence mental health;
7
8 Whereas, The Mental Health Task Force will study statutes
9 concerning matters related to mental and behavioral health and
10 methods to expand mental health services, improve referrals,
11 and develop a system;
12 Whereas, Mental health is a key component to overall
13 health and is closely linked to physical health; and
14 Whereas, The U.S. Centers for Disease Control
15 promotes a public health approach to prevent mental health
16 conditions before they develop or worsen: Therefore,
17 Be it resolved by the Senate of the

1 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
2 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate is urged to create a
