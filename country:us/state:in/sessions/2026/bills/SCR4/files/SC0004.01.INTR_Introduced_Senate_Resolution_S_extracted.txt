SENATE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. 4
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION urging the Indiana
2 Department of Transportation to rename the portion of I-69
3 North beginning at mile marker 233 the "Gregg Winters
4 Memorial Mile".
5 Whereas, Gregg William Winters was born on February 25,
6 1958, to Ezra "Zeke" and Beverly Snyder Winters in Muncie,
7 Indiana;
8 Whereas, A lifelong resident of Muncie, Gregg graduated
9 from Southside High School in 1976;
10 Whereas, Gregg worked for Wise's Food Market and
11 Motion Industries before joining the Muncie Police Department
12 in 1986 under Badge Number 167;
13 Whereas, Outside of his police work, Gregg attended
14 Shawnee Heights Baptist Church, was a member of the
15 Fraternal Order of Police, and was a devoted husband and
16 father to his wife and two young boys, Kyle and Brock;
17 Whereas, On December 28, 1990, Officer Winters was
18 dispatched to a traffic accident and observed an abandoned
19 utility truck;
20 Whereas, On the scene, officers located an intoxicated man,
21 arrested him for public intoxication, and placed him in Officer
22 Winters' police car for transport;
23 Whereas, Approximately one-eighth of a mile from the
2026 SC 4—RC 1510/DI SM
2
1 county jail, the arrestee pulled out a concealed handgun and
2 shot Officer Winters five times;
3 Whereas, Officer Winters later succumbed to his injuries on
4 January 8, 1991;
5 Whereas, Following his death, Officer Winters' wife, Molly
6 Ann Keith Winters, became an outspoken advocate for
7 survivors of officers killed in the line of duty and went on to
8 become the national president of Concerns of Police Survivors
9 and trained federal employees to help survivors of those lost
10 during the September 2001 terrorist attacks; and
11 Whereas, It is fitting to honor Officer Winters' lifelong
12 service to the Muncie community and his ultimate sacrifice
13 while in the line of duty by renaming the portion of I-69 North
14 beginning at mile marker 233 the "Gregg Winters Memorial
15 Mile": Therefore,
16 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly
17 of the State of Indiana, the House of Representatives
18 concurring:
19 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly urges the
20 Indiana Department of Transportation to rename the portion of
21 I-69 North beginning at mile marker 233 the "Gregg Winters
22 Memorial Mile".
23 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
24 to transmit copies of this Resolution to the Commissioner of the
25 Indiana Department of Transportation.
