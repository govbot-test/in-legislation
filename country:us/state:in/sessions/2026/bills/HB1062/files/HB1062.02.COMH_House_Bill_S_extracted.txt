DIGEST OF HB 1062 (Updated January 14, 2026 8:53 am - DI 140)
Citations Affected: IC 14-8; IC 14-15.
Synopsis: Wake boarding and wake surfing. Specifies restrictions that
apply to wake boarding or wake surfing on a public freshwater lake.
Establishes a penalty. Specifies restrictions that apply to wake boarding
or wake surfing on a small lake.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Abbott, Isa, Heine, Jackson C
December 5, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Natural Resources.
January 14, 2026, reported — Do Pass.

HOUSE BILL No. 1062
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
natural and cultural resources.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 14-8-2-222 IS AMENDED TO READ AS
2 FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 222. "Public freshwater
3 lake", for purposes of IC 14-15-3 and IC 14-26-2, has the meaning set
4 forth in IC 14-26-2-3.
5 SECTION 2. IC 14-8-2-301.1 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
6 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
7 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 301.1. "Wake boarding" means
8 to:
9 (1) use a short board with or without foot bindings on which
10 a rider is towed by a motorboat across a wake; or
11 (2) operate a motorboat that creates a wake and that tows or
12 is intended to tow a person who uses a short board with or
13 without foot bindings on which the person is towed by the
14 motorboat across the motorboat's wake.
15 SECTION 3. IC 14-8-2-301.4 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
16 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
17 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 301.4. "Wake surfing" means to:
HB 1062—LS 6437/DI 150
2
1 (1) surf a wake, regardless of whether the person is being
2 pulled by a tow rope attached to the motorboat that is
3 producing the wake; or
4 (2) operate a motorboat that creates a wake that is or is
5 intended to be surfed by another person.
6 SECTION 4. IC 14-15-3-8.1 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
7 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
8 1, 2026]: Sec. 8.1. An individual shall not operate a motorboat on
9 a public freshwater lake for the purpose of wake boarding or wake
10 surfing during the period between sunset and sunrise.
11 SECTION 5. IC 14-15-3-10 IS AMENDED TO READ AS
12 FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 10. (a) Subject to
13 section 11 of this chapter, a person may not operate a motorboat upon
14 a small lake at a speed greater than ten (10) miles per hour.
15 (b) An individual may not operate a motorboat upon a small
16 lake while engaging in wake boarding or wake surfing unless it is
17 during the time and day of a speed exemption granted for the small
18 lake under section 11 of this chapter.
19 SECTION 6. IC 14-15-3-11.1 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
20 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
21 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 11.1. A rule adopted under
22 section 11 of this chapter before July 1, 2026, applies to wake
23 boarding and wake surfing.
24 SECTION 7. IC 14-15-3-31, AS AMENDED BY P.L.195-2014,
25 SECTION 5, IS AMENDED TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE
26 JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 31. (a) A person who violates section 2, 3, 5, 7, 8,
27 8.1, 9, 10, 14, 15, 16, 17, 20, 22, 23, 24, 25, 26, 27, 28, 29, or 30 of this
28 chapter commits a Class C infraction.
29 (b) A person who knowingly or intentionally violates section 6 of
30 this chapter commits a Class C misdemeanor.
