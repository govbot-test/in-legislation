DIGEST OF SB 256 (Updated January 14, 2026 1:44 pm - DI 140)
Citations Affected: IC 4-6.
Synopsis: Political activity of agents of hostile nations. Requires
certain persons acting on behalf of a hostile foreign country to register
with the attorney general.

4 Chapter 17. Hostile Foreign Agent Registration
5 Sec. 1. As used in this chapter, "agent" means an agent of a
6 hostile foreign principal.
7 Sec. 2. As used in this chapter, "agent of a hostile foreign
8 principal" means a person:
9 (1) who either:
10 (A) is an agent, representative, employee, or servant; or
11 (B) acts in any capacity at the order, request, or under the
12 direction or control;
13 of a hostile foreign principal;
14 (2) whose activities are directly or indirectly financed in whole
15 or in part by a hostile foreign principal; and
16 (3) who engages in political activity in Indiana.
17 Sec. 3. As used in this chapter, "foreign supported political

8 Sec. 4. As used in this chapter, "hostile foreign country" means
9 one (1) or more of the following:
10 (1) The People's Republic of China.
11 (2) The Russian Federation.
12 (3) The People's Republic of North Korea.
13 (4) The Islamic Republic of Iran.
14 Sec. 5. As used in this chapter, "hostile foreign principal" means
15 one (1) or more of the following:
16 (1) The government of a hostile foreign country, a political
17 party of a hostile foreign country, or any member of a
18 political party of a hostile foreign country.
19 (2) A nonresident alien of a hostile foreign country.
20 (3) A partnership, association, corporation, organization, or
21 other combination of persons organized under the laws of, or
22 having its principal place of business in, a hostile foreign
23 country.
24 (4) A partnership, association, corporation, organization, or
25 other combination of persons that is at least twenty percent
26 (20%) beneficially owned by a hostile foreign country, the
27 government of a hostile foreign country, a nonresident alien
28 of a hostile foreign country, or an entity organized under the
29 laws of, or having its principal place of business in, a hostile
30 foreign country.
31 Sec. 6. As used in this chapter, "political activity" means an
32 activity that is performed with the intent to influence:
33 (1) a state or local governmental entity, agency, or public
34 official in Indiana; or
35 (2) the general public in Indiana;
36 concerning the formulation, adoption, or modification of the laws
37 or policies of Indiana, or the election or opposition to the election
38 of a candidate for state or local office.
39 Sec. 7. (a) This section applies to an agent. Before acting as an
40 agent, a person who acts as an agent shall file with the attorney
41 general a true and complete registration statement in addition to
42 supplements as required.
