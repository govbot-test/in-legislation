DIGEST OF SB 256 (Updated February 16, 2026 12:34 pm - DI 151)
Citations Affected: IC 4-6; IC 36-1.
Synopsis: Foreign agent registration and foreign terrorist
organizations. Requires certain persons acting on behalf of a hostile
foreign country to register with the attorney general. Establishes a
procedure to deny state benefits to persons who aid a foreign terrorist
organization. Provides that an agreement or cooperative agreement
between a unit and a prohibited person is contrary to public policy and
is void and unenforceable.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
