DIGEST OF INTRODUCED RESOLUTION
A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Candace Hall upon her
retirement from the Indiana Senate.
TOMES, DERNULC, DORIOT
, read first time and referred to Committee on
2026 RR 3537/DI JR
Introduced
SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Candace Hall upon
2 her retirement from the Indiana Senate.
3 Whereas, Hailing from Morgantown, Indiana, Candace
4 Hall joined the Legislative Services Agency in the summer of
5 2002;
6 Whereas, After a brief stint at LSA, Candace joined the
7 Indiana Senate as the Bookkeeper at the end of 2003, then
8 became the Assistant Majority Secretary of the Senate on July
9 7, 2013;
10 Whereas, As Assistant Majority Secretary of the Senate,
11 Candace was a lead member of the Senate Front Desk Team,
12 assisted in filing thousands of bills, resolutions, amendments,
13 and committee reports, and was a key contact for both retiree
14 support and Senate emergencies;
15 Whereas, Candace ensured the morale of members and
16 staff remained high through her dedication to the legislative
17 process and by bringing in McDonald's for the third floor every
18 third reading deadline;
2026 RR 3537/DI JR
2
1 Whereas, Outside of her time in the Senate, Candace spent
2 much of her time with her kids, parents, and grandchildren, and
3 continues to spend time with her dobermans, Raven and Rebel,
4 who are trained to do search and rescue through the American
5 Patriot K9 Search Team;
6 Whereas, On December 5, 2025, Candace retired from the
7 Indiana Senate after more than twenty years of service to spend
8 more time with family and increase her time doing search and
9 rescue; and
10 Whereas, Candace Hall is a valued member of the Indiana
11 Senate extended family and will be greatly missed: Therefore,
12 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
13 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
14 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors Candace Hall
15 upon her retirement from the Indiana Senate, and wishes her
16 and her family all the best.
17 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
18 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Candace Hall.
2026 RR 3537/DI JR
