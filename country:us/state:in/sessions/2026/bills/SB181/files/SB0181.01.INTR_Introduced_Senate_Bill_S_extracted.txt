SENATE BILL No. 181
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 32-31.
Synopsis: Notice of eviction. Requires a landlord to provide notice at
least five days before filing an eviction that includes the following: (1)
The amount owed. (2) A calculation of late fees. (3) Acceptable
methods of payment. (4) The date the landlord intends to file an
eviction if charges remain unpaid. Provides methods of written notice.
Provides that notice is not required to terminate a lease if the landlord
has met other notice requirements. Requires a landlord to complete
required repairs issued by an eligible enforcement authority and meet
standards required by a county health department. Repeals a provision
concerning refusal or neglect to pay rent and corresponding notice
requirements.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
