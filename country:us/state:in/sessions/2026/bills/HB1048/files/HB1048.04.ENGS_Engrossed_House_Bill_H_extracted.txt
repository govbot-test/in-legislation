DIGEST OF HB 1048 (Updated February 10, 2026 3:11 pm - DI 116)
Citations Affected: IC 36-8.
Synopsis: VFD clothing and automobile allowances. Increases, from
$100 to $250, the clothing and automobile allowance for an active
member of a volunteer fire department (department). Provides that a
unit served by a volunteer fire department shall pay the clothing and
automobile allowance directly to each active and participating member
of the department.
Effective: July 1, 2026.

HOUSE BILL No. 1048
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning local
government.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 36-8-12-5 IS AMENDED TO READ AS
2 FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 5. (a) A unit served by
3 a volunteer fire department shall pay directly to each active and
4 participating member of the department: Unless otherwise provided
5 by contract, a unit served by a volunteer fire department shall pay to
6 each active and participating member of the department:
7 (1) a clothing allowance of not less than one hundred dollars
8 ($100) two hundred fifty dollars ($250) per year; and
9 (2) an automobile allowance of not less than one hundred dollars
10 ($100) two hundred fifty dollars ($250) per year for the use of
11 the member's automobile in the line of duty.
12 (b) A contract may also provide that fees for membership in a
13 regularly organized volunteer firefighters' association be paid by the
14 unit on behalf of the firefighters in the volunteer fire department.
