DIGEST OF SB 211 (Updated January 20, 2026 3:27 pm - DI 149)
Citations Affected: IC 3-13.
Synopsis: Cancellation of caucus meeting. Allows a person who
wishes to be a candidate for a pro tempore appointment to certain local
offices or school board offices to file a declaration of candidacy with
the chair of the caucus or the circuit court clerk. Requires a circuit
court clerk to send a copy of a declaration of candidacy filed with the
clerk to the chairman of the caucus on the same day the declaration of
candidacy is received. Allows a notice of a caucus meeting to be
rescinded if only one candidate seeks to fill a vacancy in a local office
or a school board office held by a major party.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Holdman, Baldwin
