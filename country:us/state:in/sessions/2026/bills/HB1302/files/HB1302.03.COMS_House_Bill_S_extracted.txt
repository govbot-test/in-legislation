DIGEST OF HB 1302 (Updated February 11, 2026 1:27 pm - DI 153)
Citations Affected: IC 3-6; IC 3-11.5; IC 22-2; IC 22-9; IC 22-10.
Synopsis: Various labor and safety matters. Repeals various
provisions concerning the Indiana department of labor employer data
base for youth employment. Transfers the administration of certain age
discrimination provisions from the Indiana department of labor to the
civil rights commission. Removes provisions relating to a belt examiner
certificate. Makes corresponding changes.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
