SENATE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. _____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION memorializing Perry
2 Township Trustee Daniel Combs.
3 Whereas, Daniel (Dan) Combs passed away on January 6,
4 2026, at the age of 73;
5 Whereas, Dan Combs was born on November 26, 1952, in
6 Bloomington, Indiana, where he spent most of his life serving
7 as the trustee for Perry Township from 1986 till 2026;
8 Whereas, Dan was a writer and educator. He authored a
9 weekly column for The Herald-Times, sharing observations on
10 life, community, and parenthood with humor and candor. He
11 later returned to Indiana University, earning a degree in
12 Continuing Studies in 1994, a Bachelor of Science in Social
13 Studies Education in 1997, and, finally, a Master of Arts in
14 Teaching;
15 Whereas, Dan served in the United States Army, where he
16 trained as a medic. He also taught political science at
17 Bloomington High School South and spent a year teaching at
18 Wabash Valley Correctional Center;
19 Whereas, Dan was named Trustee of the Year by the
20 Indiana Township Association in 2015 for his commitment to
21 public service and he supported multiple community
22 organizations in and around Bloomington;
2026 DC 1901/DI ol
2
1 Whereas, Dan Combs and Perry Township staff assisted the
2 victims of the near-killer tornado that tore through southern
3 Monroe County on Friday, May 16, 2025;
4 Whereas, The EF2 tornado left a five-mile track of human
5 and property devastation through southern Monroe County,
6 from back-country Harmony Road to the Economy Inn on South
7 Walnut just outside the Bloomington city limits;
8 Whereas, The 150-foot-wide funnel destroyed the Clear
9 Creek Post Office, flattened two buildings, and severely
10 damaged dozens of houses in its path. In Perry Township alone,
11 it obliterated three quadplexes, severely damaged four brick
12 duplexes, and destroyed another three, while inflicting massive
13 damages to the Economy Inn;
14 Whereas, In the month following the tornado, Perry
15 Township staff helped 18 different households with temporary
16 housing, groceries, and other resources without denying
17 anyone assistance;
18 Whereas, Combs and Perry Township employees took the
19 entire responsibility of assisting the tornado victims with
20 housing, food, and other necessities despite having no legal
21 obligation to assist victims of natural disasters; and
22 Whereas, Dan is survived by his wife, Pat Oskam-Combs;
23 his children, Levi Combs and Erin Combs-Gillott (Simon
24 Gillott); his grandchildren, Mathis and Rowan; his siblings,
25 Pat Combs, Mike Combs (Deb Combs), Mark Combs, Deniese
26 Combs, and Angela Eads (Tony Eads): Therefore,
27 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly
28 of the State of Indiana, the House of Representatives
29 concurring:
30 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly expresses
31 its sympathy and deepest condolences to the family of Daniel
32 Combs.
2026 DC 1901/DI ol
3
1 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
2 to transmit four copies of this Resolution to the family of
3 Daniel Combs.
2026 DC 1901/DI ol
