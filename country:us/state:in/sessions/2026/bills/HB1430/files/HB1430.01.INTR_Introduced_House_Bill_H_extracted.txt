DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 3-10-9-3; IC 20-40-23; IC 20-46-10.
Synopsis: Referenda on preschool funding levies. Allows the
legislative body of a county and certain cities to adopt a resolution to
place a referendum on the ballot to impose a local preschool
referendum tax levy (levy) to fund administration of a preschool
program and provide tuition and other assistance. Allows a unit to
impose a levy if approved by a majority of the voters. Provides that
voters may not approve a levy that is imposed for more than eight
years. Specifies procedures for the referendum. Provides that a unit that
imposes a levy must establish a preschool education program and a
local preschool education referendum tax levy fund.
Effective: Upon passage.
Johnson B
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Elections and
Apportionment.
