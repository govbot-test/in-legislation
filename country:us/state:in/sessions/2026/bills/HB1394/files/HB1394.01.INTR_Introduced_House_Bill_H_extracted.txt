HOUSE BILL No. 1394
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: Noncode.
Synopsis: Social service benefit kiosk pilot program. Requires the
division of family resources (division) of the office of the secretary of
family and social services to establish a pilot program under which the
division develops, deploys, and determines the effectiveness of
electronic kiosks that allow an individual to do any of the following: (1)
Determine the balance remaining in the individual's SNAP or TANF
account. (2) Apply to the family and social services administration for
a new EBT card. (3) Apply for SNAP or TANF benefits, subject to
verification through in person communication with the division.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Summers
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Family, Children and Human
Affairs.
