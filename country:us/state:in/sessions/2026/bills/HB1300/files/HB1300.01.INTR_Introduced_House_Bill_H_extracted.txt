HOUSE BILL No. 1300
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 36-8-8.5.
Synopsis: Deferred retirement option plan. Allows a member of the
1925 police pension fund, 1937 firefighters' pension fund, 1953 police
pension fund (Indianapolis), or 1977 police officers' and firefighters'
pension and disability fund to withdraw from the deferred retirement
option plan (DROP) and make an election to enter the DROP for a
second time not earlier than three years after the date the member
withdraws from the DROP. Provides that a member may make an
election to enter the DROP only twice in the member's lifetime.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Smith V
January 6, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Employment, Labor and
Pensions.
