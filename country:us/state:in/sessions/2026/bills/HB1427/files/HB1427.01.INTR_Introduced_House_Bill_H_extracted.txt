HOUSE BILL No. 1427
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 25-1-23.
Synopsis: Reporting on gender transition procedures. Requires
practitioners who perform gender transition procedures to report on a
quarterly basis certain information to the practitioner's regulating board
concerning the gender transition procedures. Requires the submitted
reports to be provided to the professional licensing agency (agency) to
be compiled as an annual report. Requires the agency to post the annual
report on the agency's website and submit the report to the general
assembly.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Smith H, Barrett, McGuire,
Carbaugh

4 Chapter 23. Reporting on Gender Transition Procedures
5 Sec. 1. The definitions in IC 25-1-22 apply to this chapter.
6 Sec. 2. As used in this chapter, "practitioner" has the meaning
7 set forth in IC 25-1-9-2.
8 Sec. 3. (a) A practitioner who performs any gender transition
9 procedure (as defined in IC 25-1-22-5) shall report the
10 performance of the procedures on a quarterly basis to the
11 practitioner's regulating board.
12 (b) The report by the practitioner under subsection (a) must
13 include the following:
14 (1) The county in which each gender transition procedure was
15 performed.
16 (2) The type of gender transition procedure performed.
17 (c) Each board that receives a report under this section shall
