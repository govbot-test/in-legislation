HOUSE BILL No. 1250
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 11-10-12-2.5; IC 35-38-1; IC 35-40-6-4.
Synopsis: Notice of release and victim notification. Requires the
department of correction to electronically notify, at least seven days in
advance, the: (1) county sheriff; (2) prosecuting attorney; and (3) chief
of police; of the county or municipality into which the department will
release a serious violent felon, and of the county or municipality where
the serious violent felon committed the offense. Requires: (1) a court;
and (2) the prosecuting attorney or the prosecuting attorney's victim
assistance program; to notify a victim of the automated victim
assistance notification program.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
O'Brien, McNamara, Bascom
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Courts and Criminal Code.
