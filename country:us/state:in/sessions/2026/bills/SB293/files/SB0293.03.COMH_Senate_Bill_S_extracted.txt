DIGEST OF SB 293 (Updated February 17, 2026 11:32 am - DI 147)
Citations Affected: IC 25-26; noncode.
Synopsis: Pharmacist licensure. Removes an examination requirement
for certain pharmacists for license reciprocity. Requires an individual
to pass the North American Pharmacist Licensure Examination to be
eligible for licensure as a pharmacist. Voids or requires the amendment
of certain administrative rules. Makes technical changes.
Effective: Upon passage.
