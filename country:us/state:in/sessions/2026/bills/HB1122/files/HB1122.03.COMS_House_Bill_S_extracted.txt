DIGEST OF HB 1122 (Updated February 17, 2026 9:33 am - DI 140)
Citations Affected: IC 9-18.5.
Synopsis: License plates for former state legislators. Provides that a
special license plate may be issued to former members of the general
assembly who served: (1) for a former member of the house of
representatives, not less than five terms; (2) for a former member of the
senate, not less than three terms; and (3) for an individual who served
in both the house of representatives and the senate, for a total of not
less than 12 years.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Bartlett, Soliday, Davis
(SENATE SPONSORS — NIEMEYER, DERNULC, TAYLOR G)
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Roads and Transportation.
January 27, 2026, amended, reported — Do Pass.
January 29, 2026, read second time, ordered engrossed.
January 30, 2026, engrossed.

Transportation.
February 17, 2026, reported favorably — Do Pass.

HOUSE BILL No. 1122
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
motor vehicles.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 9-18.5-3-2 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
2 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
3 1, 2026]: Sec. 2. A special license plate may be issued to a former
4 member of the general assembly who served not less than the
5 following:
6 (1) For a former member of the house of representatives, not
7 less than five (5) terms.
8 (2) For a former member of the senate, not less than three (3)
9 terms.
10 (3) If an individual was a member of both the house of
11 representatives and the senate, for a total of not less than
12 twelve (12) years.
