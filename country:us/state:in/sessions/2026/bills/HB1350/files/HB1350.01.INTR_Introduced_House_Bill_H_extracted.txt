HOUSE BILL No. 1350

DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 35-37-4-16; IC 35-38-4-2.
Synopsis: Exclusionary rule. Prohibits the exclusion of otherwise
admissible evidence based on a violation of the Constitution of the
State of Indiana, the Constitution of the United States, or the laws of
the United States or Indiana unless certain conditions apply. Provides
that an appeal from a court's order excluding evidence may be taken
directly to the supreme court.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Shonkwiler
January 6, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Courts and Criminal Code.
