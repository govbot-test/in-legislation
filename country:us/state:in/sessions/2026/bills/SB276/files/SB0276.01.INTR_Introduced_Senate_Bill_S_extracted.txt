DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 16-35-1.7.
Synopsis: Farmers' market nutrition assistance programs. Requires,
subject to federal approval of any necessary plan, amendment, or
waiver, the Indiana department of health (state department) to provide
for: (1) a recipient of benefits under the federal Supplemental Nutrition
Assistance Program (SNAP) to receive triple value for the recipient's
SNAP benefits; and (2) a recipient of benefits under the federal Special
Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children
Program (WIC) to receive triple value for the recipient's WIC benefits;
when used to purchase produce at a farmers' market that participates in
one or more specified federal programs administered by the state
department.
Effective: Upon passage.
Ford J.D., Pol Jr.
January 12, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Family and Children
Services.

A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
health.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 16-35-1.7 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
2 AS A NEW CHAPTER TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE
3 UPON PASSAGE]:
4 Chapter 1.7. Farmers' Market Nutrition Programs
5 Sec. 1. As used in this chapter, "SNAP" has the meaning set
6 forth in IC 12-7-2-178.9.
7 Sec. 2. As used in this chapter, "WIC" has the meaning set forth
8 in IC 16-35-1.5-2.
9 Sec. 3. (a) Subject to subsection (b), the state department shall
10 provide for:
11 (1) an Indiana resident who receives SNAP benefits to receive
12 triple value for the recipient's SNAP benefits when used to
13 purchase produce at a farmers' market that participates in a
14 program funded in whole or in part through the federal Gus
15 Schumacher Nutrition Incentive Program, including:
16 (A) the Double Up Indiana program; and
17 (B) the Fresh Bucks program; and

1 (2) an Indiana resident who receives WIC benefits to receive
2 triple value for the recipient's WIC benefits when used to
3 purchase produce at a farmers' market that participates in
4 the federal:
5 (A) WIC Farmers' Market Nutrition Program; or
6 (B) Senior Farmers' Market Nutrition Program;
7 through price discounts, matching benefits, or a combination of
8 price discounts and matching benefits.
9 (b) If the state department determines that a plan amendment,
10 plan waiver, or other authorization by the United States
11 Department of Agriculture is necessary to implement subsection
