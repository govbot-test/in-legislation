MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION congratulating Marian
2 University on its 175th anniversary.
3 Whereas, Marian University was founded by the Sisters of
4 St. Francis of Oldenburg and for generations has provided
5 mission driven, high quality Catholic higher education in
6 Indiana;
7 Whereas, Marian University's vision and mission
8 emphasize preparing transformative leaders for service to the
9 world and advancing excellence in teaching, learning,
10 research, and scholarly activity in the Franciscan and liberal
11 arts tradition;
12 Whereas, Marian University has contributed to the state
13 through distinctive signature programs, workforce
14 development, community health initiatives, and K-12 outreach;
15 Whereas, Marian University has demonstrated sustained
16 enrollment growth, a strong record of research and grant
17 activity, a commitment to expanding access for students across
18 Indiana through graduate and online programs, and ongoing
19 community engagement that strengthens neighborhoods and the
