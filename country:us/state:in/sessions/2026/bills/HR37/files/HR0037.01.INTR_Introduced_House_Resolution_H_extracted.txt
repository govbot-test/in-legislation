1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION honoring Vincente Lorenz, an
2 educator at Kokomo High School, for his distinguished service
3 as a teacher and the accolades he has earned.
4 Whereas, Vincente Lorenz has served as a distinguished
5 science educator at Kokomo High School since 2012, where he
6 has been a pillar of the Wildkat faculty and a trusted mentor to
7 countless students;
8 Whereas, Mr. Lorenz specializes in a broad range of
9 scientific disciplines, including Earth Science, Astronomy,
10 Meteorology, Physical and Historical Geology, and
11 International Baccalaureate Environmental Systems and
12 Societies;
13 Whereas, Mr. Lorenz's teaching philosophy is informed by
14 a global academic background, including the completion of a
15 Master of Science in Science Education through Montana State
16 University's Nepal Educational Connection, an immersive
17 program conducted in the Himalayan Mountains that
18 integrated geology, biology, physics, and sociology;
