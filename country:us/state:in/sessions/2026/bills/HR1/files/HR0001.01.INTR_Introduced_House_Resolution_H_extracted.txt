1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION honoring John Kautzman.
2 Whereas, John Kautzman passed away on October 20, 2025,
3 at the age of 66;
4 Whereas, John was a lifelong Indianapolis resident and
5 attended North Central High School. He graduated from
6 Indiana University in 1981 and the Indiana University Robert
7 H. McKinney School of Law in 1984;
8 Whereas, Upon graduation from law school, he joined the
9 Ruckelshaus Kautzman Blackwell law firm, where he worked
10 for 40 years. His focus was on both state and federal criminal
11 and civil litigation, trying more than 100 first chair jury trials;
12 Whereas, John spent his career working with the Fraternal
13 Order of Police, advocating for and defending police officers,
