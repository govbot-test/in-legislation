MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION to honor the Professionals of
2 the Internet Crimes Against Children Task Force.
3 Whereas, The Indiana Internet Crimes Against Children
4 (ICAC) Task Force is a multi-agency task force, overseen by the
5 Indiana State Police, that investigates and prosecutes persons
6 who use the internet to sexually exploit or entice children;
7 Whereas, In 1998, the United States Office of Juvenile
8 Justice and Delinquency Prevention (OJJDP) established ICAC
9 to help local and state law enforcement combat the significant
10 increase in technology-facilitated child exploitation;
11 Whereas, The Indiana ICAC Task Force encourages
12 Hoosiers to report online exploitation, solicitation, and
13 enticement-type crimes against children to the National Center
14 for Missing & Exploited Children; and
15 Whereas, In 2025, the Indiana ICAC Task Force processed
16 22,501 reports, adding to the national total of 89,400 people
17 arrested as a result: Therefore,
18 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
2026 DR 3834/DI aj

2
1 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
2
3 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors the
4 professionals of the Internet Crimes Against Children Task
5 Force and thanks them for their service.
6 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is directed to
7 transmit copies of this Resolution to the Indiana State Police.
