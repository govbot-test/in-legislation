HOUSE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. ____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION congratulating Michelin
2 North America, Inc., upon the occasion of the 65th anniversary
3 of its BFGoodrich tire production facility in Fort Wayne,
4 Indiana.
5 Whereas, The BFGoodrich tire production facility in Fort
6 Wayne, Indiana, will be celebrating its 65th anniversary in
7 2026;
8 Whereas, Michelin initially produced removable pneumatic
9 tires for bicycles and horse-drawn carriages. They introduced
10 automobile tires in the 1890s, setting the company on the path
11 to becoming one of the world's iconic tire manufacturers;
12 Whereas, BFGoodrich Company was founded in 1870,
13 manufactured the first U.S. pneumatic tire in 1896, and in 1976
14 created the world's first radial all-terrain performance tire,
15 launching the all-terrain category;
16 Whereas, BFGoodrich broke ground on its tire production
17 facility in Fort Wayne in 1960 and produced its first tire on
18 September 20, 1961;
19 Whereas, The twentieth century marked a period of
20 significant product innovation, growth, and demand. Michelin
21 began expanding into new global markets, including North
22 America;
23 Whereas, Michelin acquired BFGoodrich in 1990, aligning
24 complementary product portfolios;

5 of hours to support the communities where its associates live
6 and work;
7 Whereas, The company's economic growth has benefited
8 many generations of workers through thriving manufacturing
9 careers;

15 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives

18 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly
