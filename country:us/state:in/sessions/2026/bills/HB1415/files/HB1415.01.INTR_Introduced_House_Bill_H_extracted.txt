DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 32-30-6.
Synopsis: Limits on nuisance actions against racetracks. Provides that
a racing facility or racetrack or any of its appurtenances is not and does
not become a nuisance, public or private, within the area of the racing
facility or racetrack, in an action brought by a person who purchased
or improved property within the area of the racing facility or racetrack
or its appurtenances after the racing facility or racetrack initially
commenced operation as a racing facility or racetrack.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Baird, Commons
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Local Government.

HOUSE BILL No. 1415
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
property.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 32-30-6-1.3 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
2 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
3 1, 2026]: Sec. 1.3. As used in this chapter, "area of the racing
4 facility or racetrack" means the area located within a five (5) mile
5 radius of the perimeter of the property or contiguous group of
6 properties operated as a racing facility or racetrack.
7 SECTION 2. IC 32-30-6-4.5 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
8 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
9 1, 2026]: Sec. 4.5. As used in this chapter, "racing facility or
10 racetrack" means a designated area or facility where competitive
11 vehicle and motorsport races are conducted. The term includes the
12 track, spectator areas, garages, and any associated grounds,
13 buildings, or appurtenances used to operate the races.
14 SECTION 3. IC 32-30-6-8.5 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
15 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
16 1, 2026]: Sec. 8.5. (a) This section does not apply if a nuisance
17 results from the negligent operation of a racing facility or

1 racetrack or the racing facility's or racetrack's appurtenances.
2 (b) A racing facility or racetrack or any of its appurtenances is

20 operation of a racing facility or racetrack in a manner inconsistent
21 with subsection (b).
22 (e) This section may not be construed to exempt a racing facility
23 or racetrack from compliance with federal, state, or local health or
24 safety laws or ordinances.
