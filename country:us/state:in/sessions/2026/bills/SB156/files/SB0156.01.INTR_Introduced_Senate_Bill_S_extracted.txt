DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 35-38-1-7.1.
Synopsis: Dating app aggravator. Makes it an aggravating
circumstance for purposes of sentencing that the person: (1) committed
a sex offense; and (2) used an online platform designed for dating to
meet the victim.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Bohacek
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Corrections and Criminal
Law.
