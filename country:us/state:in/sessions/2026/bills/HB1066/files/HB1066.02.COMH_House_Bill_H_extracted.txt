DIGEST OF HB 1066 (Updated January 21, 2026 12:36 pm - DI 87)
Citations Affected: IC 5-22.
Synopsis: Purchase and lease of government vehicles. With certain
exceptions, requires a political subdivision to procure only base model
vehicles. Removes a provision allowing the Indiana department of
administration to give an exception to a state entity from the
requirement of procuring a base model vehicle. Requires state entities
and political subdivisions to dispose of vehicles that are not base model
vehicles not later than: (1) July 1, 2028; or (2) July 1 of the calendar
year following the calendar year in which the vehicle reaches the end
of its useful life; whichever is later.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
