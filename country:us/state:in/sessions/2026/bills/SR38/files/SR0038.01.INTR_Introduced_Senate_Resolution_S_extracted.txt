MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Senator Kyle Walker
2 upon his retirement from the Indiana Senate.
3 Whereas, Senator Kyle Walker has faithfully and honorably
4 served the constituents of Senate District 31, encompassing
5 portions of Hamilton and Marion counties, since he was
6 caucused in to fill out the remainder of a term in 2020;
7 Whereas, During his tenure, Senator Walker has served as
8 ranking member of the Senate Committees on Insurance and
9 Financial Institutions and Public Policy, and as a member of
10 the Senate Committees on Tax and Fiscal Policy, Corrections
11 and Criminal Code, and Education;
12 Whereas, Some measures that Senator Walker is most
13 proud of are the change in the state's local income tax
14 distribution formula to ensure the City of Fishers receives a
15 more equitable share of local income tax revenue, securing
16 funding for the Internet of Things Lab, and the creation of a
17 professional sports development area for Fishers;
18 Whereas, Senator Walker also authored the Sports and
19 Tourism Bid Fund to help communities across Indiana access

6 information on firearms used in violent crime, as well as
7 increase law enforcement agency communication, improve
8 dispatch systems and public safety response times, and enhance
9 the penalties for swatting;
10 Whereas, One of his major focuses has been foster youth,
11 exemplified with the passage of Senate Enrolled Act 246 in
12 2022 and his work on Senate Bill 15 this session;
