HOUSE BILL No. 1252
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
professions and occupations.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 25-34.1-10-18 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
2 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 18. (a) A broker company who
4 refers a client or customer to another broker company shall
5 disclose to the client or customer if the broker company may be
6 compensated for referring the client or customer.
7 (b) A disclosure under subsection (a) must be made:
8 (1) in writing; and
9 (2) at the time of the referral.

Mr. Speaker: Your Committee on Employment, Labor and Pensions,
