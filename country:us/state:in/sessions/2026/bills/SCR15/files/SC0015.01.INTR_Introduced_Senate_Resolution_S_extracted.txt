SENATE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. _____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION congratulating the
2 Hamilton Southeastern High School boys lacrosse team for
3 winning the 2025 Indiana High School Lacrosse Association
4 state championship.
5 Whereas, On May 31, 2025, the Hamilton Southeastern
6 (HSE) High School boys lacrosse team defeated Carmel High
7 School in the Indiana High School Lacrosse Association Class
8 2A boys state championship;
9 Whereas, HSE won their second consecutive state title,
10 while continuing their streak of making the finals in each of the
11 seven previous years;
12 Whereas, Led by head coach Scott Beesley since 2014,
13 along with assistance from Tim Shefferly, HSE scored 312
14 goals, while only conceding 143 in their 16-5-0 season;
15 Whereas, Senior defenseman Dillon O'Rourke was named
16 the IHSLA Player of the Year and was also named an All-
17 American with teammates David Gould, Kyle Jenkins, and Max
18 Kemp, while goalie Luke Williams was named an Academic All-
19 American; and
20 Whereas, This year's title was HSE's fifth in the past seven
21 years, further exemplifying their dominance in lacrosse:
22 Therefore,
23 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly
24 of the State of Indiana, the House of Representatives
2026 RC 1523/DI JR
2
1 concurring:
2 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly
3 congratulates the Hamilton Southeastern High School boys
4 lacrosse team for winning the 2025 Indiana High School
5 Lacrosse Association state championship.
6 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
7 to transmit copies of this Resolution to the Hamilton
8 Southeastern High School boys lacrosse team.
2026 RC 1523/DI JR
