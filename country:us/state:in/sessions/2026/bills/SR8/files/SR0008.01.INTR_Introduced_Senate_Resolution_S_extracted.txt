Introduced
SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION recognizing the partnership
2 between Quebec, Canada, and the State of Indiana.
3 Whereas, The State of Indiana and the Province of Quebec
4 have long enjoyed friendly relations, our economies mutually
5 benefitting from each other;
6 Whereas, Quebec and Indiana, along with other states,
7 provinces, and cities participating in the Great Lakes-St.
8 Lawrence Conference, the Great Lakes-St. Lawrence
9 Legislative Caucus, and the Great Lakes and St. Lawrence
10 Cities Initiative, place significant importance similar issues and
11 shared goals;
12 Whereas, Quebec is Indiana's second-largest trading
13 partner among Canadian provinces;
14 Whereas, Over the past five years, trade between Quebec
15 and Indiana has steadily increased;
16 Whereas, Quebec is the third-largest international buyer of
17 Indiana's motor vehicles and the third-largest international
18 buyer for motor cars and parts of motor vehicles;
2026 RR 3514/DI JR
2
1 Whereas, Quebec is home to more than 20 Hoosier
2 businesses, and Indiana is home to more than a dozen Quebec-
3 owned companies; and
4 Whereas, Indiana and Quebec have a history of being
5 strong partners through issues related to water, trade, and the
6 environment: Therefore,
7 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
8 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
9 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate recognizes the
10 partnership between Quebec and Indiana.
11 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
12 to transmit copies of this Resolution to the Consulate General
13 of Canada in Chicago.
