DIGEST OF SB 281 (Updated February 11, 2026 5:45 pm - DI 125)
Citations Affected: IC 5-28; IC 6-3.1; IC 36-7; IC 36-7.6.
Synopsis: Income tax credits. Requires the Indiana economic
development corporation (IEDC) to commit $35,000,000 in
redevelopment tax credits each state fiscal year among development
authorities, qualified nonprofit organizations, and certain local
economic development organizations that may be granted to taxpayers
for qualified investments. Provides that the IEDC and an operating
partner shall administer the federal Unmanned Aircraft System Test
Site program in Indiana. Requires that $15,000,000 of the
$300,000,000 of the IEDC's annual certifiable tax credit amount must
be allocated to the small town opportunity initiative (initiative).
Establishes the initiative. Provides that initiative projects are not
subject to any statutory or administrative repayment obligation.
