HOUSE BILL No. 1181
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 2-5-55.
Synopsis: Eviction task force. Establishes the access to counsel in
eviction task force (task force) to review matters related to the eviction
process and potential funding sources to increase a tenant's access to
counsel in an eviction proceeding. Sets forth membership, and requires
the task force to issue a report to the legislative council. Provides that
the task force expires December 31, 2026.
Effective: Upon passage.
Shackleford
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Judiciary.
