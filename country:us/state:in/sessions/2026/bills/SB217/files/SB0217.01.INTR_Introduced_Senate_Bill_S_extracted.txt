SENATE BILL No. 217
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 6-1.1-53.5.
Synopsis: Circuit breaker credit for homesteads. Limits the year over
year increase in property tax liability for homesteads to not more than
4%.
Effective: January 1, 2026 (retroactive).
Buck, Niemeyer
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Tax and Fiscal Policy.
