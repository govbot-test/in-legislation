HOUSE BILL No. 1209
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 36-1.
Synopsis: Municipal collection protocols. Effective January 1, 2027,
establishes requirements for the provision of invoices and delinquency
notices for certain municipal accounts. Requires the state board of
accounts to prescribe forms for an invoice and a notice of a delinquent
account. Requires municipal officers and employees with oversight
responsibilities or access to the municipality's financial payment
system to be segregated from the responsibilities of reporting or
recording payments received. Requires a municipal officer whose
official duties include oversight of reporting, recording, or monitoring
of the municipality's accounts to not have access to the officer's own
personal account.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Olthoff
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Local Government.
