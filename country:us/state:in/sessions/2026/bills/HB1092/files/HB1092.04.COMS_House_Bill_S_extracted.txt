Synopsis: Child services matters. Requires a sports wagering
certificate holder and vendor to withhold funds from a person who
requests a certain type of withdrawal from the person's mobile sports
wagering account if the person is delinquent in the person's child
support payments. Requires an order establishing paternity to be
obtained without further proceedings if certain conditions are met.
Allows the state to approve additional payment facilities for purposes
of accepting child support payments. Provides that if a child who is the
