DIGEST OF HB 1092 (Updated January 21, 2026 1:00 pm - DI 119)
Citations Affected: IC 4-38; IC 31-14; IC 31-16; IC 31-19; IC 31-27;
IC 31-30; IC 31-33.
Synopsis: Child services matters. Requires a sports wagering operator
to withhold winnings from a person who requests a withdrawal from
the person's mobile sports wagering account if the person is delinquent
in the person's child support payments. Requires an order establishing
paternity to be obtained without further proceedings if certain
conditions are met. Allows the state to approve additional payment
facilities for purposes of accepting child support payments. Makes
changes to the adoption code to facilitate communication between a
probate court and a juvenile court when the subject of an adoption
petition is also the subject of a pending child in need of services
(CHINS) petition. Provides that if a child who is the subject of an
adoption petition is also the subject of a CHINS petition: (1) the clerk
of the court in the adoption proceeding shall forward a copy of the
adoption petition to the department of child services (department) if the
child is in the care and custody of the department; and (2) the
department shall provide notice of the adoption proceeding to the court
in the CHINS proceeding. Provides that the department has the right to
intervene as a party to an adoption proceeding under certain
