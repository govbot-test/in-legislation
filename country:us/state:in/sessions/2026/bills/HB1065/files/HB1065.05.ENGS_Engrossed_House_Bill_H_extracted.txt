HOUSE BILL No. 1065

DIGEST OF HB 1065 (Updated February 23, 2026 5:24 pm - DI 106)
Citations Affected: IC 3-14.
Synopsis: Ban on gratuities for public officials. Provides that a person
who knowingly or intentionally violates a specified provision by
commingling the funds of a committee with the personal funds of an
officer, a member, or an associate of the committee commits a Class A
misdemeanor. Enhances the penalty to a Level 6 felony if a person
commingles at least $50,000 of committee funds.
Effective: July 1, 2026.

3 recklessly violates IC 3-9-2-9(c) by commingling the funds of a
