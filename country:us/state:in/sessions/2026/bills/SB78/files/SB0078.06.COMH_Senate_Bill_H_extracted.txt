DIGEST OF SB 78 (Updated February 18, 2026 2:39 pm - DI 134)
Citations Affected: IC 10-21; IC 20-26; IC 34-30.
Synopsis: Wireless communication device policy. Amends definitions,
requirements, restrictions, and exceptions regarding wireless
communication device policies adopted and implemented by school
corporations and charter schools. Requires the department of education
to publish model policy language and implementation guidance.
Provides that, with exceptions, a school corporation, a school
maintained by a school corporation, and a charter school, including
school personnel, are immune from civil liability for any actions taken
in good faith to comply with the school's wireless communication
device policy.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
