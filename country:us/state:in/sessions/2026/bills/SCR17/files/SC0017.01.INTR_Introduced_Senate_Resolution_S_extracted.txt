RESOLUTION No. _____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION memorializing Michael
2 David Nance.
3 Whereas, Michael (Mike) Nance passed away on November
4 8, 2025, at the age of 71;
5 Whereas, Mike was born on August 14, 1954, in
6 Providence, Kentucky, where he spent most of his early days
7 before moving to Indianapolis, Indiana;
8 Whereas, Mike graduated from Madisonville North
9 Hopkins High School, where he sparked his interest in music by
10 becoming a member of the marching band and where he was an
11 active member of FFA;
12 Whereas, Mike graduated from the University of Southern
13 Indiana with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and
14 obtained his CPA license, working as a bank auditor for
15 years;
16 Whereas, Mike served on the Wayne Township School
17 Board for thirty-one years, serving as an officer for thirteen
18 of those years, always wanting to help and lend a hand. He
19 was the Indiana School Board Association Legislative
20 Liaison for many of those years, working hard to improve his
21 schools and community;
2026 DC 1900/DI ol
2
1 Whereas, Mike was the owner of the UPS Store in
2 Speedway for fourteen years alongside his late wife, Roberta
3 Nance;
4 Whereas, Mike will be remembered for his kind heart,
5 infectious laughter, and his unwavering dedication for those
6 he cared about; and
7 Whereas, Mike is survived by his daughter Becky
8 (Courtney Harwell) Nance and his son Corey (Jennifer)
9 Nance; his four grandchildren, Ryleigh, Evangeline,
10 Jameson, and Zariyah; and his uncles, cousin, and sister-in-
11 law: Therefore,
12 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly
13 of the State of Indiana, the House of Representatives
14 concurring:
15 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly
16 expresses its sympathy and deepest condolences to the family
17 of Michael David Nance.
18 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby
19 directed to transmit two copies of this Resolution to the
20 family of Michael David Nance.
