DIGEST OF HB 1249 (Updated February 10, 2026 1:05 pm - DI 106)
Citations Affected: IC 31-30; IC 35-47.
Synopsis: Juvenile court jurisdiction. Specifies that a child charged
with dangerous possession of a firearm may be tried by a juvenile court
under certain circumstances. Makes dangerous possession of a firearm
a Level 5 felony if the child: (1) has a prior conviction for unlawful
carrying of a handgun; or (2) possesses the firearm on school property,
within 500 feet of a school, or on a school bus.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
