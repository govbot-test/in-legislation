HOUSE BILL No. 1170
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 20-28-9-26.
Synopsis: Teacher compensation. Provides that, for each school year
beginning after June 30, 2027, a school corporation shall, if the school
corporation determines that the school corporation cannot establish a
minimum salary of $60,000 (instead of $40,000 under current law) for
each full-time teacher, comply with certain requirements.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Klinker
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Education.
