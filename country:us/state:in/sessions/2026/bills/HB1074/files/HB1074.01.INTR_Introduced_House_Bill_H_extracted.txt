DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 12-15-1.3-26.1.
Synopsis: Nutrition supports Medicaid waiver. Requires the office of
the secretary of family and social services to apply, not later than
October 1, 2026, for approval of a Section 1115 Medicaid
demonstration waiver or any other Medicaid waiver to provide
coverage for nutrition supports for specified individuals.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Andrade, Shackleford, Ledbetter
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Health.

HOUSE BILL No. 1074
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
human services.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 12-15-1.3-26.1 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
2 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 26.1. (a) As used in this section,
4 "nutrition supports" includes the following:
5 (1) Case management services.
6 (2) Nutrition counseling and instruction.
7 (3) Home delivered meals, including medically tailored meals,
8 or pantry stocking.
9 (4) Nutrition prescriptions.
10 (5) Grocery provisions.
11 (b) Not later than October 1, 2026, the office of the secretary
12 shall apply to the United States Department of Health and Human
13 Services for approval of a Section 1115 Medicaid demonstration
14 waiver or any other Medicaid waiver to provide Medicaid coverage
15 that includes nutrition supports for the following Medicaid
16 recipients:
17 (1) Medicaid recipients with chronic diseases that are
