26 2026, within the geographic boundaries of the shooting range

28 Sec. 3. As used in this chapter, "indoor shooting range" means
29 a fully enclosed facility designed and operated for the discharge of
30 firearms, equipped with a bullet resistant backstop, ventilation
31 system, and sound attenuation measures to prevent the escape of
32 projectiles, excessive noise, or airborne contaminants.
33 Sec. 4. As used in this chapter, "mixed use residential" means a
34 development project that provides within a shared building or
35 development area:
36 (1) residential uses, including multiple dwelling units; and
37 (2) nonresidential uses that:
38 (A) comprise less than fifty percent (50%) of the total
39 square footage of the development; and
40 (B) are restricted to the first floor of any building
41 consisting of at least two (2) stories.
42 Sec. 5. As used in this chapter, "permitted use" means a use that
