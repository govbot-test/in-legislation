DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 20-19-3-41.3.
Synopsis: School corporation report. Requires the department of
education to prepare a report compiling statewide data on school
corporations and submit the report to the legislative council and the
interim study committee on education by September 1, 2026.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Yocum
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Education.
2026 IN 1242—LS 6896/DI 143
Introduced
Second Regular Session of the 124th General Assembly (2026)
PRINTING CODE. Amendments: Whenever an existing statute (or a section of the Indiana
Constitution) is being amended, the text of the existing provision will appear in this style type,
additions will appear in this style type, and deletions will appear in this style type.
Additions: Whenever a new statutory provision is being enacted (or a new constitutional
provision adopted), the text of the new provision will appear in this style type. Also, the
word NEW will appear in that style type in the introductory clause of each SECTION that adds
a new provision to the Indiana Code or the Indiana Constitution.
Conflict reconciliation: Text in a statute in this style type or this style type reconciles conflicts
between statutes enacted by the 2025 Regular Session of the General Assembly.
HOUSE BILL No. 1242
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
education.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 20-19-3-41.3 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
2 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 41.3. (a) The department shall
4 prepare a report compiling statewide data on school corporations.
5 The report must include the following information:
6 (1) The total number of school corporations in Indiana
7 disaggregated by:
8 (A) county population;
9 (B) the number of school corporations per county; and
10 (C) the number of students enrolled in each school
11 corporation.
12 (2) Recommendations to:
13 (A) increase school corporation administrative efficiencies;
14 and
15 (B) reduce school corporation administrative costs.
16 (b) Not later than September 1, 2026, the department shall, in
17 an electronic format under IC 5-14-6, submit the report prepared

2
1 under subsection (a) to the:
2 (1) legislative council; and
3 (2) interim study committee on education established by
4 IC 2-5-1.3-4.
5 (c) This section expires December 31, 2026.
