Second Regular Session 124th General Assembly (2026)
SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Senator J.D. Ford
2 upon his retirement.
3 Whereas, Senator J.D. Ford came from humble beginnings,
4 graduating as a first-generation college student, earning a
5 Bachelor of Science in Political Science and Criminal Justice
6 from the University of Akron and a Master of Science in
7 Education with a focus on Human Services from Purdue
8 University;
9 Whereas, Senator J.D. Ford has worked in higher
10 education and the nonprofit sector, where he has held executive
11 leadership roles dedicated to strengthening communities across
12 Central Indiana;
13 Whereas, Since 2018, Senator J.D. Ford has proudly served
14 District 29, which includes parts of Boone, Hamilton, and
15 Marion counties;
16 Whereas, Senator J.D. Ford made Indiana history when
17 elected, becoming the first and only out member of the
18 LGBTQ+ community to serve in the Indiana General Assembly;
2026 DR 3848/DI aj
2
1 Whereas, Throughout Senator J.D. Ford's tenure, he has
2 served as Ranking Minority Member on the Senate Committees
3 on Elections and Education and Career Development and as a
4 valued member of the Senate Committees on Family and
5 Children Services, Ethics, Health and Provider Services,
6 Veterans Affairs and The Military, Commerce and Technology,
7 Homeland Security and Transportation, and Utilties;"
8 Whereas, Senator J.D. Ford has worked tirelessly to meet
9 the needs of his constituents, including authoring legislation to
10 assist homeless youth, protect election integrity, and increase
11 transparency for Alzheimer's and dementia care;
12 Whereas, Outside legislative work, Senator J.D. Ford is
13 actively involved in the community he serves, attending local
14 graduation events, recognizing his constituents publicly, and
15 working in various capacities in his community; and
16 Whereas, Senator J.D. Ford has been acknowledged as the
17 2023 Stonewall Champion, the 2022 Friend of Public
18 Education by the Indiana State Teachers Association and, the
19 2020 Advocate of School Social Work by the Indiana School
20 Social Work Association, and received the Legislative Award
21 from the Indiana Friends Committee: Therefore,
22 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
23 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
24
25 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors Senator J.D.
26 Ford for his dedication and service.
27 SECTION 2. That the Secretary of the Senate is directed to
28 transmit copies of this Resolution to Senator J.D. Ford.
