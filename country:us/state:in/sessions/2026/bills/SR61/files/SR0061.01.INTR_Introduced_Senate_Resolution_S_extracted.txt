MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION memorializing Dr. Gerardo
2 Gomez.
3 Whereas, Dr. Gerardo Alfredo Gomez Losada was born on
4 April 13, 1946, in Barquisimeto, Venezuela, and passed away
5 at age 74 in Indianapolis, Indiana, on Thursday, August 6,
6 2020;
7 Whereas, Already a respected student and community
8 member after his graduation from medical school at
9 Universidad de Carabobo in Venezuela, Dr. Gomez immigrated
10 to America and completed his surgical residency at the
11 University of Massachusetts, Worcester;
12 Whereas, After Dr. Gomez completed his residency, he
13 started his career as a trauma surgeon in 1982 at Jackson
14 Memorial Hospital in Miami, Florida, and eight years later, he
15 became the Chief of Trauma Surgery at Wishard Hospital,
16 Indianapolis, as a faculty member at Indiana University;
17 Whereas, This was the beginning of Dr. Gomez's 26-year
18 career serving the community of Indianapolis, showing that you
19 do not need to be born a Hoosier to become one;

1 Whereas, Throughout Dr. Gomez's career, he mentored
2 thousands of students, doctors, nurses, and paramedics, taking
3 great pride in these relationships;
4 Whereas, Dr. Gomez saved countless lives and strived to
5 make a difference in the community that he loved;
6 Whereas, Dr. Gomez enjoyed educating the next generation
7 by teaching courses in Advanced Trauma Life Support and
8 Advanced Trauma Operative Management around the world;
9 Whereas, Dr. Gomez was a member and former president
10 of the Pan-American Trauma Society, and known as a father
11 figure for trauma care in the state of Indiana; and
12 Whereas, Aside from Dr. Gomez's accomplishments, he

16 Liam; granddaughters Alexandra and Bailee; and
