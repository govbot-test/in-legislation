Synopsis: Bureau of motor vehicles. Provides that the supplemental
fee for electric and hybrid vehicles does not apply to low speed
vehicles. Allows additional license plates issued by the bureau to be
designed as a personalized license plate. Provides that a motor vehicle
may be equipped with an appropriate, functioning rear view camera
that provides the driver a view of the highway for a distance of at least
200 feet to the rear of the vehicle instead of a mirror. Amends the
information required to be provided by an applicant for a driver's
license or permit. Amends the required age an individual must be to
obtain a driver's license from 16 years and 90 days of age to 16 years
of age. Requires an individual to demonstrate sufficient proficiency of
the English language to operate a commercial motor vehicle. Provides
that an individual commits a Level 6 felony if the individual: (1)
represents that a false record is a valid commercial driver's license; or
(2) does not have proper documentation while driving with a
commercial driver's license issued by any jurisdiction other than a
