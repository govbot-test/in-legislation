DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 8-1-2-16.
Synopsis: Electric generation asset reports. Prescribes additional
information that an electricity supplier must include in the electricity
supplier's annual accounting filing with the Indiana utility regulatory
commission.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Doriot
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Utilities.

SENATE BILL No. 168
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
utilities.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 8-1-2-16 IS AMENDED TO READ AS FOLLOWS
2 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 16. (a) The accounts shall be closed
3 annually on the thirty-first day of December, and a balance sheet of that
4 date promptly taken therefrom. On or before the thirtieth day of April
5 following, such balance sheet, together with such other information as
6 the commission shall prescribe, verified by an officer of the public
7 utility, shall be filed with the commission.
8 (b) An electricity supplier, as defined by IC 8-1-2.3-2(b), shall
9 include in the electricity supplier's filing under subsection (a) a
10 separate summary of the following information for each of the
11 electricity supplier's electric generation plants:
12 (1) The original book value of the generation plant.
13 (2) The current book value of the generation plant.
14 (3) The total amount of investments made in the generation
15 plant for the purpose of compliance with federal
16 requirements.
17 (4) The estimated replacement cost of the generation plant on

1 an accredited basis using the generation plant's summer
2 UCAP (as defined by IC 8-1-8.5-13(k)).
3 (c) The commission may issue a general administrative order to
4 prescribe:
5 (1) any information supplementary to the information under
6 subsection (b); and
7 (2) any additional information that the commission considers
8 necessary;
9 that an electricity supplier must submit in the electricity supplier's
10 filing under subsection (a).
