SENATE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. _____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION congratulating the
2 Fishers Junior High School "We the People" team on winning
3 the 2025 "We the People" middle school title.
4 Whereas, On December 9, 2025, the Fishers Junior High
5 School "We the People" team took first place in the state
6 competition as the top middle school across the state of
7 Indiana;
8 Whereas, The team will have the opportunity to compete in
9 the "We the People" National Invitational, which will take
10 place May 15-16, 2026;
11 Whereas, During the competition, teams of students
12 answered questions from panels of judges about the
13 Constitution and its application to contemporary issues;
14 Whereas, Involvement with the "We the People"
15 competition allows students to develop a greater understanding
16 of democratic principles of our constitutional republic and
17 prepares Indiana youth for a future that will encourage their
18 knowledge of and participation in our democratic system of
19 government; and
20 Whereas, The "We the People" program was developed by
21 the Center for Civic Education and is funded by the State of
22 Indiana and the Indiana Bar Foundation, which administers the
23 state "We the People" program to promote civic responsibility
24 and competence in students: Therefore,
2026 RC 1520/DI JR
2
1 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly
2 of the State of Indiana, the House of Representatives
3 concurring:
4 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly
5 congratulates the Fishers Junior High School "We the People"
6 team on winning the 2025 "We the People" middle school title.
7 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
8 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Jude Troxell, Rowan
9 Basham, Avalyn Chau, Charles Haider, David Kolic, Josh Lim,
10 Jude Bozarth, Guneet Bajwa, Nate Cherry, Andrew Nguyen,
11 Gwendolyn Johnson, Clara Davis, Rubani Chhokar, Quinn
12 Tesch, Mahita Gopalakrishnan, Evan Dean, Nazi Mostafa,
13 Kendalynn McDowell, Brynlee Likens, Amelia Smith, Heather
14 Butz, and Tony Sturgeon.
