HOUSE BILL No. 1414
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 14-23.
Synopsis: Forest management. Requires the department of natural
resources (department) to establish recreational fees for the access and
use of state forest land. Provides that the department shall ensure that
at all times: (1) 10% of state forest land consists of forests that are 100
years or older; and (2) 10% of state forest land consists of forests that
are 20 years or younger. Provides that money in the state forestry fund
does not revert to the state general fund.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Baird
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Natural Resources.
