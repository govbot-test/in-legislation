MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION recognizing National
2 Engineers Week.
3 Whereas, National Engineers Week, celebrated annually in
4 February, highlights the meaningful contributions engineers
5 make to our communities, economy, and quality of life;
6 Whereas, The theme of National Engineers Week, which
7 celebrates its 75th anniversary in 2026, emphasizes innovation,
8 problem-solving, and service to society, which are values that
9 align with Indiana's consulting engineers working in
10 transportation, energy, water resources, and infrastructure;
11 Whereas, The American Council of Engineering Companies
12 of Indiana (ACEC Indiana) represents consulting firms across
13 the state that design, build, and maintain the critical systems
14 and structures that keep Indiana moving forward;
15 Whereas, For more than 20 years, as part of National
16 Engineers week, ACEC Indiana and its member firms have
17 participated in classroom visits and educational outreach
18 throughout the state to introduce students to the engineering
19 profession, encourage STEM education, and inspire young
