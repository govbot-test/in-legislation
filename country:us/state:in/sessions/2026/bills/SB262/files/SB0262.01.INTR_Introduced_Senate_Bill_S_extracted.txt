SENATE BILL No. 262
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 25-26-24.
Synopsis: INSPECT program. Amends the definition of "dispense" for
purposes of the INSPECT program. Allows the board of pharmacy to
disclose prescription drug monitoring program data to a representative
of the Indiana department of health and the office of the secretary of
family and social services in certain circumstances.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Crider
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Health and Provider
Services.
