SENATE BILL No. 120
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 4-38.
Synopsis: Proposition bets. Prohibits a certificate holder or vendor
from accepting a proposition bet from a collegiate athlete in the sport
the collegiate athlete plays. Provides that the Indiana gaming
commission may impose a civil penalty upon an individual who: (1) is
a collegiate athlete; and (2) attempted to place or placed a proposition
bet on a game in the sport that the individual plays.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Zay
December 9, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Policy.
