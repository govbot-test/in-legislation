SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION recognizing the partnership
2 between Alberta, Canada, and the State of Indiana.
3 Whereas, The State of Indiana and the Province of Alberta
4 have long enjoyed friendly relations, our economies mutually
5 benefitting from each other;
6
7 Whereas, Canadians have played a large role in Indiana's
8 economy, with 22,935 jobs being supported by trade with
9 Alberta, and bilateral trade between Indiana and Alberta
10 totaled $645.3 million in 2023;
11 Whereas, Alberta accounts for 56 percent of U.S. crude oil
12 imports, while Canada provides 99 percent of the input
13 imported by Midwest refineries, including the BP refinery in
14 Whiting, Indiana;
15 Whereas, Indiana's top exports to Alberta include motor
16 vehicles and parts, machinery, aluminum, energy, soap, and
17 washing preparations;
18 Whereas, Stantec, a global design and engineering firm
19 headquartered in Edmonton, Alberta, has four locations in
2026 RR 3512/DI JR
2
1 Indiana, and has worked on projects such as an affordable
2 housing development in Culver and developing a native plant
3 nursery in Walkerton, which has provided millions of native
4 plants and seeds for restoration projects for over 25 years;
5 Whereas, INCA Renewtech, a company that uses Canadian
6 hemp biomass leftover from protein production, creates hemp-
7 based composite panel products, some of which get sent to
8 INCA's factory in Bristol, Indiana, to manufacture panels for
9 the RV industry; and
10 Whereas, Indiana and Alberta have a history of being
11 strong partners and trusted neighbors through bilateral trade,
12 integrated economies, and similar energy approaches:
13 Therefore,
14 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
15 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
16 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate recognizes the
17 partnership between Alberta and Indiana.
18 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
19 to transmit copies of this Resolution to the Alberta Midwest
20 Office co-located in the Consulate General of Canada in
21 Chicago.
