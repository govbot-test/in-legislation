HOUSE BILL No. 1078
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 4-30.
Synopsis: Online lottery sales and voluntary exclusion. Authorizes the
lottery commission (commission) to operate the sale of draw games and
eInstant games over the Internet. Provides that unless granted authority
by the general assembly, the commission shall not operate or authorize
the use of certain digital lottery games. Requires the commission to
adopt rules to implement and establish a voluntary exclusion program.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Manning
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Policy.
