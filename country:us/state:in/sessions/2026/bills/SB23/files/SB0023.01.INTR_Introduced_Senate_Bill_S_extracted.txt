DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 7.1-3-21-14.5.
Synopsis: Alcoholic beverage permits for county fairs. Allows the
alcohol and tobacco commission to issue a permit for the sale of
alcoholic beverages on the grounds of a county fair conducted by a
local board, subject to certain limitations and requirements.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Clark
December 8, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Policy.

SENATE BILL No. 23
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
alcohol and tobacco.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 7.1-3-21-14.5 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
2 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 14.5. (a) As used in this section,
4 "local board" means the board of directors of an agricultural fair
5 society, association, or corporation that is organized under
6 IC 15-14-5.
7 (b) The commission may issue a permit for the sale of alcoholic
8 beverages on the grounds of a county fair conducted by a local
9 board to the local board, if one (1) of the following applies:
10 (1) If the grounds of the county fair are owned by a local
11 board, upon the request of the local board.
12 (2) If the grounds of the county fair are owned by a unit (as
13 defined in IC 36-1-2-23), upon the approval of the legislative
14 body (as defined in IC 36-1-2-9) of the unit and upon the
15 request of the local board.
16 (c) A permit issued under subsection (b) is subject to the
17 following:
2026 IN 23—LS 6146/DI 137
2
1 (1) The permit is valid for the scheduled dates of the county
2 fair.
3 (2) The local board is entitled to sell alcoholic beverages on
4 the county fair grounds to consumers by the glass.
5 (3) The local board is entitled to permit multiple vendors with
6 separate permits at different locations on the county fair
7 grounds to sell alcoholic beverages to consumers by the glass.
8 (4) The permit is not subject to the quota restrictions under
9 IC 7.1-3-22-3.
10 (5) The local board is entitled to allow a minor to be present
11 in the locations on the county fair grounds where alcoholic
12 beverages are sold.
13 (d) A local board issued a permit under subsection (b) must
14 comply with the following requirements:
15 (1) File a floor plan of the premises where alcoholic beverages
16 will be served and consumed with the commission.
17 (2) Provide that service of alcoholic beverages may be
18 performed only by servers certified under IC 7.1-3-1.5.
19 (3) Allow sales during the times prescribed under
20 IC 7.1-3-1-14.
21 (4) Prohibit sales prohibited under IC 7.1-5-10-1.
22 (5) Operate under the rules adopted by the commission to
23 protect the public interest under IC 7.1-1-1.
