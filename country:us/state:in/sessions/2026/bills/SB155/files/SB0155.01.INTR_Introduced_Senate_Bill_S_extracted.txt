SENATE BILL No. 155
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
human services.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 12-8-1.5-22.5 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
2 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 22.5. The office of the secretary
4 shall develop and implement a doula program that includes at least
5 the following:
6 (1) Recruitment.
7 (2) Eligibility requirements, such as certification or
8 registration.
9 (3) Training requirements.
10 (4) A doula certification body.
11 (5) An enrollment process for a doula to receive Medicaid
12 reimbursement during the twelve (12) months of a
13 postpartum period described in IC 12-15-2-13, including
14 reimbursement for telehealth services.
15 (6) Client outreach.
16 (7) A process for matching a doula to a pregnant Medicaid
17 recipient.
