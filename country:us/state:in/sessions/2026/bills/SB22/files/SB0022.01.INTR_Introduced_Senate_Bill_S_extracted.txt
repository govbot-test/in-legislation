SENATE BILL No. 22
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 5-2-6.1-1.2; IC 6-1.1-1-4.7; IC 10-14-1-3.5;
IC 12-7-2-51.3; IC 16-18-2-88.4; IC 23-14; IC 25-1; IC 25-15; IC 29-2;
IC 30-2-13; IC 35-31.5-2-72.7; IC 36-1-2.
Synopsis: Dissolution of human remains. Expands the definition of
"cremation" to include alkaline hydrolysis as a means for the
dissolution of human remains. Requires the state board of funeral and
cemetery service to adopt rules governing the operation of alkaline
hydrolysis facilities. Adds alkaline hydrolysis to statutes that allow for
burial or cremation. Specifies that a person may request alkaline
hydrolysis in statutes that allow for burial or cremation.
Effective: Upon passage; July 1, 2026.
Clark
December 8, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Policy.
