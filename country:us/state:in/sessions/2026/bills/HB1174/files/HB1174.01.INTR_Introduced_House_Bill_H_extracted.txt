HOUSE BILL No. 1174
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 12-9-5-3; IC 36-8-29.
Synopsis: Project Lifesaver program requirement for counties.
Requires each county to establish and maintain a Project Lifesaver
program. Tasks the division of disability and rehabilitative services
with ensuring compliance, and requires the division to report to the
general assembly.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Pfaff, Andrade
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Local Government.

10 Chapter 29. Project Lifesaver Program
11 Sec. 1. As used in this chapter, "Project Lifesaver" means the
12 community based, public safety, nonprofit organization that
13 provides:
14 (1) law enforcement agencies;
15 (2) fire and rescue agencies; and
16 (3) caregivers;
17 with a program designed to protect and locate an individual with
18 a cognitive disorder who is prone to wandering behavior.
19 Sec. 2. Not later than January 1, 2027, a county shall establish
20 and maintain its own Project Lifesaver program.
21 Sec. 3. The county fiscal body of a county shall appropriate and
22 approve the money necessary for the county to comply with this
23 chapter.
24 Sec. 4. (a) The division of disability and rehabilitative services
25 shall ensure that a county complies with this chapter.
26 (b) The division of disability and rehabilitative services shall
27 report, in an electronic format under IC 5-14-6, county compliance
28 to the general assembly not later than March 1, 2027.
29 (c) The division of disability and rehabilitative services may
30 adopt rules under IC 4-22-2 to carry out this chapter.
