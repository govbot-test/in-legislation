SENATE BILL No. 263
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 6-3.1-37.
Synopsis: Child and dependent care tax credit. Provides a refundable
child and dependent care tax credit to taxpayers whose adjusted gross
income for the taxable year is not more than 250% of the federal
poverty level. Provides that the credit is equal to the lesser of: (1) an
amount ranging from $200 to $1,000, depending on the extent to which
the taxpayer's adjusted gross income exceeds the federal poverty level;
or (2) 20% of the taxpayer's employment related expenses.
Effective: January 1, 2026 (retroactive).
Ford J.D.
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Tax and Fiscal Policy.
