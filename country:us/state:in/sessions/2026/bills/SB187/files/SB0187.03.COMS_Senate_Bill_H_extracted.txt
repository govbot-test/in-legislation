DIGEST OF SB 187 (Updated January 20, 2026 11:49 am - DI 129)
Citations Affected: IC 13-11; IC 13-26.
Synopsis: Regional sewer districts. Specifies that a board of trustees
of a regional water, sewage, or solid waste district (board) must notify
the district authority in writing before taking certain actions. Provides
that a trustee appointed to a board serves at the pleasure of the
appointing authority. Prohibits a regional water, sewage, or solid waste
district (district) created after June 30, 2026, from incurring any
indebtedness without first obtaining the approval of the county fiscal
body. Specifies that if a district created after June 30, 2026, contains
territory in more than one county, the district must first obtain the
approval of each county fiscal body in which the district contains
territory before incurring any indebtedness. Establishes a timeline for
the county fiscal body to approve or deny a district's request to incur
indebtedness.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
