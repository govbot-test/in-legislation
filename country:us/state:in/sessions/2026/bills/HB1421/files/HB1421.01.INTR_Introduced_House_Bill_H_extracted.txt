DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 22-5-10.4.
Synopsis: Ban on employer use of automated decision systems.
Prohibits an employer from: (1) relying exclusively on an automated
decision system in making an employment related decision with respect
to a covered individual; (2) using an automated decision system output
in making an employment related decision with respect to a covered
individual unless certain conditions are met; and (3) discriminating or
retaliating against a covered individual for exercising rights under these
provisions. Sets forth disclosure requirements for an employer that uses
or intends to use an automated decision system output in making an
employment related decision. Allows the department of labor to take
certain enforcement actions. Allows a covered individual or labor
organization to bring a civil action for a violation.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
