December 4, 2025
Second Regular Session of the 124th General Assembly (2026)
HOUSE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. 1
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION urging the Indiana
2 Department of Transportation to rename the U.S. 41 bridge
3 over the Kankakee River the "Corporal Brandon Schreiber
4 Memorial Bridge".
5 Whereas, Corporal Brandon W. Schreiber was born October
6 16, 1991, in Crown Point, Indiana, to Joseph and Lorraine
7 Schreiber;
8 Whereas, Corporal Schreiber was a 2010 graduate of
9 Hanover Central High School where he played baseball and
10 ran cross country;
11 Whereas, Corporal Schreiber joined the Newton County
12 Sheriff's Department full time in 2019 after serving as a deputy
13 for three years;
14 Whereas, Corporal Schreiber died on September 11, 2024,
15 after being shot in the line of duty during a vehicle pursuit 10
16 days prior;
17 Whereas, Corporal Schreiber was an avid outdoorsman who
18 enjoyed hunting, fishing, and shooting at the range, and he
19 loved spending time with his family. He is survived by his wife,
20 Kaitlin, and their two children, Seth and Paisley; and
21 Whereas, Corporal Schreiber selflessly served the people of
22 Newton County and gave his life protecting his community:
23 Therefore,
HC 1—HC 1003/DI 92

2
1 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives
2 of the General Assembly of the State of Indiana,
3 the Senate concurring:
4 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly urges the
5 Indiana Department of Transportation to rename the U.S. 41
6 bridge over the Kankakee River the "Corporal Brandon
7 Schreiber Memorial Bridge".
8 SECTION 2. That the Principal Clerk of the House of
9 Representatives shall transmit copies of this resolution to the
10 Commissioner of the Indiana Department of Transportation.
