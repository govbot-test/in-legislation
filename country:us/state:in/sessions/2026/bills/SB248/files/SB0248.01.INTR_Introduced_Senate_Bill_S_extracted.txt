DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 20-23.
Synopsis: South Bend Community school corporation. Provides that
members of the governing body for the South Bend Community school
corporation (school corporation) serving on June 30, 2026, shall
become on July 1, 2026, an advisory board to the governing body for
the school corporation. Provides that, beginning July 1, 2026, members
of the governing body for the school corporation shall be appointed by
the secretary of education following a nomination process. Establishes
a commission to nominate individuals for the governing body.
Effective: Upon passage.
Rogers, Raatz, Mishler, Donato
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Education and Career
Development.

14 serve as a member of the South Bend Community school
15 corporation advisory board pursuant to IC 20-23-20-5 until the

17 under IC 20-23-20-6.

6 Sec. 1. As used in this chapter, "commission" means the
7 governing body nominating commission established by section 7 of

9 Sec. 2. As used in this chapter, "fiscal body" has the meaning set
10 forth in IC 36-1-2-6.
11 Sec. 3. IC 20-23-8 does not apply to the South Bend Community

14 Sec. 4. (a) Beginning July 1, 2026, the governing body of the

16 members appointed by the secretary of education from a list of
17 individuals nominated under section 9 of this chapter.
18 (b) An individual appointed under this section serves a four (4)
19 year term.
20 (c) A member of the governing body serves at the pleasure of the
21 appointing authority.
22 (d) A member of the governing body may be reappointed to
23 successive terms.
24 Sec. 5. The governing body serving on June 30, 2026, shall
25 become on July 1, 2026, an advisory board to the governing body
26 described in section 4 of this chapter. The members of the

28 become the members of the advisory board. Each member shall
29 serve on the advisory board until the term for which the member
30 was elected before July 1, 2026, expires or until the term
31 terminates under section 6 of this chapter. When the terms of all
32 members of the advisory board have expired, or terminated under
33 section 6 of this chapter, the advisory board is abolished. The
34 advisory board shall not possess any of the power and duties of the
35 governing body. A member of the advisory board may receive
36 compensation for serving on the advisory board.

38 by the secretary of education to one (1) of the positions of the
39 governing body described in section 4 of this chapter, the following
40 apply:
41 (1) An individual seeking a nomination for appointment by
42 the secretary of education to the governing body shall submit

18 subdivision (3) to the secretary of education for consideration
19 for appointment under section 4 of this chapter not later than
20 June 1, 2026, and before June 1 of each year thereafter in
21 which the term of an individual described in section 4 of this
22 chapter expires.
23 Sec. 10. (a) A vacancy in the office of a member of the governing
24 body shall be filled as soon as practicable after the vacancy occurs
25 in the same manner by the appointing authority. The commission
26 shall nominate at least three (3) individuals per vacancy to the
27 secretary of education for consideration for appointment. An
28 individual filling a vacancy under this subsection serves until the
29 expiration of the term of the member whose position the individual
30 fills.
31 (b) A vacancy in the office of a member of the commission shall
32 be filled in the same manner by the appointing authority that
33 appointed the member whose office is vacant.
34 SECTION 4. An emergency is declared for this act.
