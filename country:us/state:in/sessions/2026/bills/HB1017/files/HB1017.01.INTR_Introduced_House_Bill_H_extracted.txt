DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 21-27-11.
Synopsis: University notification of emergency contact. Requires each
postsecondary educational institution in Indiana and state educational
institution to: (1) establish and implement a procedure whereby a
student may designate a medical emergency contact for medical
emergencies that occur on campus; and (2) if the institution has
knowledge of the medical emergency, notify the student's medical
emergency contact after the student experiences a medical emergency
on campus.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Garcia Wilburn
December 1, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Education.

A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
higher education.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 21-27-11 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
2 AS A NEW CHAPTER TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE
3 JULY 1, 2026]:
4 Chapter 11. Notification of Medical Emergency Contact
5 Sec. 1. As used in this chapter, "medical emergency" means an
6 incident concerning a student's health that results in the
7 notification and deployment of emergency services.
8 Sec. 2. As used in this chapter, "student" means a student who
9 is:
10 (1) at least eighteen (18) years of age; and
11 (2) enrolled in a postsecondary educational institution in
12 Indiana or a state educational institution as a full-time
13 student.
14 Sec. 3. (a) Subject to subsection (b), each postsecondary
15 educational institution in Indiana and state educational institution
16 shall do the following:
17 (1) Establish and implement a procedure whereby a student

7 (2) Not later than twenty-four (24) hours after a student
