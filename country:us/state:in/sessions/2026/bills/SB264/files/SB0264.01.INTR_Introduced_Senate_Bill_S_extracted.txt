DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 6-3.1-13.
Synopsis: Economic development tax credits. Amends the economic
development for a growing economy (EDGE) tax credit to: (1)
authorize the Indiana economic development corporation (IEDC) to
increase the value of an EDGE credit to a company for new job
creation based on the amount of expenses of the company to relocate
an individual to Indiana to fill the position; and (2) explicitly permit the
IEDC to provide EDGE credits to a company that is retaining an
employee through a minimum of a 25% increase in hourly wages paid
to the individual.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Buchanan, Rogers
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Tax and Fiscal Policy.
