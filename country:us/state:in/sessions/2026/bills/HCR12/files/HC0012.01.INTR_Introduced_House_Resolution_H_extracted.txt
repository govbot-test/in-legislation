Second Regular Session of the 124th General Assembly (2026)
HOUSE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. ____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION honoring Cindy Hoye
2 for her longstanding service to the Indiana State Fair
3 Commission.
4 Whereas, Cindy Hoye has worked for the Indiana State Fair
5 Commission for almost 40 years;
6 Whereas, Cindy Hoye became executive director in 2004 after
7 serving 16 years as the commission's marketing director;
8 Whereas, Under her leadership, the commission oversaw the
9 renovation of the Corteva Coliseum and the construction of the
10 Indiana Farm Bureau Fall Creek Pavilion, as well as
11 expansions of 4-H programming;
12 Whereas, Cindy Hoye additionally helped attract high profile
13 track and field events, including the NCAA Division II National
14 Championships, Big Ten Championships, and the Hoosier State
15 Relays, to Indianapolis and the fairgrounds campus; and
16 Whereas, Cindy Hoye plans to retire from her position as
17 executive director in March of this year: Therefore,
18 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives
19 of the General Assembly of the State of Indiana,
20 the Senate concurring:
21 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly honors
22 Cindy Hoye for her longstanding service to the Indiana State
23 Fair Commission.
24 SECTION 2. That the Principal Clerk of the House of
2026 HC 1009/DI 140
2
1 Representatives shall transmit copies of this resolution to State
2 Representative Gregory Porter for distribution.
