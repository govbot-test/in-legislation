SENATE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. _____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION congratulating the
2 Avon Marching Black and Gold.
3 Whereas, The Avon High School Marching Band has a
4 history of excellence in competitions, including winning the
5 Indiana State School Music Association (ISSMA) state finals
6 eighteen times and the Bands of America Grand National
7 Championship six times;
8 Whereas, The esteemed history of excellence includes
9 participating in events such as the Rose Bowl Parade in 2012
10 and Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade in 2024;
11 Whereas, Led by Matt Harloff, Matt Abbey, Karl Hartman,
12 Jeremy Basso, Daniel Wiles, and Lee Allman, the 2025
13 Marching Black and Gold program was titled "Axiom";
14 Whereas, The Marching Black and Gold excelled in their
15 2025 competitions, winning first place or gold medals at every
16 event, before competing and winning the ISSMA state finals on
17 November 8, 2025; and
18 Whereas, Following their state title, the Marching Black
19 and Gold also won the Bands of America Grand National
20 Championships on November 15, 2025, for the third
21 consecutive year: Therefore,
22 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly
23 of the State of Indiana, the House of Representatives
24 concurring:
2026 RC 1526/DI JR
2
1 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly
2 congratulates the Avon Marching Black and Gold on their
3 recent successes in the state marching band championship and
4 the Bands of America Grand National Championships.
5 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
6 to transmit a copy of this Resolution to the Avon Marching
7 Black and Gold.
2026 RC 1526/DI JR
