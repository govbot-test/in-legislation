1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION recognizing the seriousness of
2 the US national debt and its threat to national security.
3 Whereas, in December 2025, the outstanding public debt of
4 the United States totaled more than $38 trillion, with public
5 debt equaling 118 percent of gross domestic product and the
6 debt owed per citizen rising $112,000;
7 Whereas, More than $8 trillion of the federal debt is owned
8 by individuals not located in the United States, including more

4 budget and federal tax receipts totaled $5 trillion, short of the
5 federal outlays totaling $6.75 trillion;
6 Whereas, the Social Security and Medicaid Board of
7 Trustees project that the Old-Age and Survivors Insurance
8 Trust Fund, and the Federal Hospital Insurance Trust Fund
9 will all be depleted in 2033 and 2036;
10 Whereas, improvements in the business climate in populous
11 countries and aging populations around the world will likely
12 contribute to higher global interest rates, which would squeeze
13 all U.S. policy priorities, including defense and foreign policy;
14 Whereas, on September 22, 2011, former Chairman of the
15 Joint Chief of Staff Michael Mullen warned, “the single,
16 biggest threat to our national security is debt”;
17 Whereas, on March 6, 2018, the Director of National
18 Intelligence Dan Coats warned, “our continued plunge into
19 debt is unsustainable and represents a dire future threat to our
20 economy and to our national security”;
21 Whereas, the last federal budget surplus occurred in 2001
22 and the continued rise in the national debt is a major concern
23 across Indiana and around the country: Therefore,
24 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the
25 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
25 SECTION 1. That the State of Indiana recognizes that the
26 national debt is a threat to the national security of the United
27 States and urges the United States Congress to commit to
28 establishing an effective regular order for budgeting.
29 SECTION 2. The Clerk of the House is directed to transmit
30 a copy of this Resolution to each member of Indiana’s
31 Congressional delegation.
