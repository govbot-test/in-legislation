DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 33-28-1-2; IC 33-29; IC 33-31-1-9;
IC 33-34-3-3.
Synopsis: Marion County small claims court jurisdiction. Requires the
following cases to be filed in a Marion County small claims court: (1)
A possessory action between a landlord and a tenant in which the past
due rent does not exceed $10,000. (2) An action for the possession of
property where the value of the property does not exceed $10,000.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Taylor G
December 11, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Judiciary.
