1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION celebrating the 50th anniversary
2 of the City of Carmel and the Carmel City Council.
3 Whereas, Carmel's transition to a city began with a pivotal
4 vote by residents in November 1974, following state legislation
5 that allowed towns to be designated fourth-class cities. After
6 the first municipal election in 1975, the inaugural council
7 members took their seats exactly 50 years ago on January 5;
8 Whereas, On January 5, 2026, the Carmel City Council
9 recognized the inaugural Common Council's seven members,
10 including David Coots, Dr. Walter Dean, Minnie Doane, James
11 Garretson, Stan Meacham, Jane Reiman, and Fred Swift;
12 Whereas, These original councilors played a crucial role
13 during the City of Carmel's critical transition years,
14 establishing essential services and infrastructure that
