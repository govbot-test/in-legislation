SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring Sergeant Bill
2 Spalding upon his retirement.
3 Whereas, William K. Spalding has served as an employee
4 of the Indiana State Police since July 1989, after graduating
5 from Indiana University with a Bachelor's degree in public
6 affairs and a minor in psychology;
7 Whereas, After being assigned to the Lowell District as
8 Probationary Trooper, Spalding was then assigned to the
9 Seymour District as a Trooper;
10 Whereas, In 1999, Spalding was promoted to Corporal
11 while serving the Pendleton District, and two years later was
12 promoted to Sergeant;
13 Whereas, Spalding transferred to the Indianapolis District
14 in 2010, and in 2018 was given a temporary duty assignment in
15 State Police's legal department to provide security for the
16 President Pro Tempore;
17 Whereas, An ever-present figure around the Senate,
18 Spalding has endeared himself amongst Senators, staff, and all
2026 RR 3535/DI JR

2
1 who interact with him;
2 Whereas, Outside of work, Spalding is a member of Zion
3 Lutheran Church in New Palestine, Indiana, has three sons,
4 Ethan, Jacob, and Joey, and enjoys reading, gardening,
5 working on old cars, and beekeeping; and
6 Whereas, Sergeant Bill Spalding is a valued member of the
7 Indiana Senate extended family and will be greatly missed:
8 Therefore,
9 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
10 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
11 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate honors Sergeant Bill
12 Spalding upon his retirement, and wishes him and his family all
13 the best.
14 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
15 to transmit copies of this Resolution to Sergeant Bill Spalding.
