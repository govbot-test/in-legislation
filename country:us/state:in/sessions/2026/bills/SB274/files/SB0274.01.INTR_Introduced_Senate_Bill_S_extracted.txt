SENATE BILL No. 274
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 36-4-3-19.1.
Synopsis: Disannexation of property. Provides that disannexation of
certain property is effective upon filing a disannexation petition with
the county auditor and recording the petition with the county recorder.
Requires the property owner to file a petition requesting a municipality
to annex the property not more than 48 months after the disannexation
is effective.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Niemeyer
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Local Government.
