SECTION 5. IC 21-22-6-4, AS AMENDED BY P.L.174-2018,
SECTION 21, IS AMENDED TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE
JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 4. A vacancy on the campus board shall be filled
by appointment by the state board of trustees. The campus board, in
collaboration with the campus chancellor, shall nominate and submit
to the state board the names of one (1) or more candidates to fill the
vacancy within ninety (90) days after the vacancy occurs. The state
board of trustees may appoint one (1) of the persons nominated by the
campus board or may reject all of the nominees. If the state board of
trustees rejects all of the nominees, the state board of trustees shall
notify the campus board, and the campus board shall make one (1) or
more additional nominations within ninety (90) days after receipt of the
notice. The state board of trustees shall then fill the vacancy from either
the original group of nominations or from the additional nominations.
