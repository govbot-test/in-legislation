1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring the 2026 Senate

3 Regular Session of the One Hundred Twenty-Fourth Indiana
4 General Assembly.
5 Whereas, Members of the 2026 intern class include:
6 Gabrielle Alford, Andrew Carles Hilger, Shailey Desai, Tina
7 El-Amoor, Daniel Huber, Sanjana Kakanur, Oluwasola Lasaki,
8 Jordan McAtee, Shaina Miller, Josephine Morrison, El'ad
9 Nichols-Kaufman, Mike Obioma, and Neil Sahu;
10 Whereas, The interns of the Senate Democratic Caucus
11 represent eight different universities and colleges: Indiana
12 University Indianapolis, Indiana University Bloomington,
13 Butler University, Ball State University, Purdue University,
14 DePauw University, St. John's College Annapolis, and The
15 United States Military Academy;
16 Whereas, The interns serve in all departments assisting the
17 caucus senators, caucus attorney, and staff while
18 demonstrating dedication, professionalism, and strong
19 commitment to public service rooted in integrity and humility;

1 Whereas, They have shown true teamwork and adaptability
2 which enabled senators of the Democratic Caucus to better
3 represent and serve constituents in a prompt, significant,
4 reliable, and respectful manner;
5 Whereas, The interns' exemplary work ethics, hardwork,
6 and diligence has contributed to the overall success of the
7 Democratic Caucus and their curiosity and willingness to grow
8 has reflected the true purpose of service; and
9 Whereas, The work of the Democratic interns is inherent
10 to the success of the Indiana General Assembly and the state of
11 Indiana: Therefore,
12 Be it resolved by the Senate of the
13 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
14 SECTION 1. That the Indiana Senate expresses its deep
15 appreciation to the Senate Democratic Caucus interns.
16 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
17 to transmit fourteen copies of this Resolution to the Senate
18 Democratic Caucus interns.
