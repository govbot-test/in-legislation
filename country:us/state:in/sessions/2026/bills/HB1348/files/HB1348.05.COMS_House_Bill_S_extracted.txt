DIGEST OF HB 1348 (Updated February 17, 2026 9:57 am - DI 140)
Citations Affected: IC 8-1; IC 13-11; IC 13-18.
Synopsis: Regulation of grease control equipment. Establishes a
statewide regulatory scheme for grease control equipment. Provides
that a waste water treatment plant must continue to accept septage
under certain circumstances. Creates a process for septage haulers to
resolve disputes with wastewater treatment plants with respect to
accepting septage.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
