DIGEST OF HB 1029 (Updated February 11, 2026 9:51 am - DI 140)
Citations Affected: IC 16-41.
Synopsis: Alzheimer's disease and dementia education. Requires the
Indiana department of health (state department) to: (1) collaborate with
a national Alzheimer's disease and dementia organization in educating
the public about Alzheimer's disease and dementia; and (2) identify and
collaborate with additional partners in the education. Requires the state
department to partner for outreach in the education and publish certain
educational materials on the state department's website. Allows the
state department to accept grants, services, and property from public
and private entities for the education.
Effective: July 1, 2026.

1 SECTION 1. IC 16-41-18.2 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
2 AS A NEW CHAPTER TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE
3 JULY 1, 2026]:
4 Chapter 18.2. Alzheimer's Disease and Dementia Education
5 Sec. 1. (a) The state department shall collaborate with a national
6 Alzheimer's disease and dementia organization in educating the
7 public about the following concerning Alzheimer's disease and
8 dementia:
9 (1) The signs and symptoms of Alzheimer's disease and
10 dementia.
11 (2) Personal risk factors.
12 (3) Options for diagnosing and treating Alzheimer's disease
13 and dementia.
14 (4) Populations with elevated risk for Alzheimer's disease and
15 dementia.
16 (b) The state department shall identify additional partners to
17 collaborate with in educating the public about Alzheimer's disease

2 (1) Local health departments.
