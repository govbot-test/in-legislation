DIGEST OF HB 1342 (Updated January 21, 2026 11:52 am - DI 144)
Citations Affected: IC 1-1; IC 14-13; IC 36-2.
Synopsis: County commissioner districts. Requires, after December
31, 2030, the county executive to: (1) adopt an ordinance to divide the
county into three districts that meet certain standards; and (2) publish
proposed district boundaries and hold a public hearing before an
ordinance is adopted. Provides exceptions. Specifies that the county
executive may consider geography and the differences in population
between each district when dividing the county into districts.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
