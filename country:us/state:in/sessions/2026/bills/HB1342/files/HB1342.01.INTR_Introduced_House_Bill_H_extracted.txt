HOUSE BILL No. 1342
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 3-5-10; IC 36-2-2-4.
Synopsis: County commissioner districts. Requires the county
executive in certain counties to either redraw or recertify county
commissioner districts not later than December 31, 2027. Provides that
county commissioner districts in certain counties must contain, as
nearly as is possible, equal population.
Effective: January 1, 2027.
Yocum
January 6, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Elections and
Apportionment.
