RESOLUTION No. _____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION honoring Chesterton
2 Large Co-ed Cheer on their State Title.
3 Whereas, The Chesterton High School Large Co-ed Cheer
4 team (The Trojans) won the Indiana Association of School
5 Principals (IASP) Indiana Cheer Championship on Saturday,
6 November 8, 2025 in New Castle;
7 Whereas, The Indiana Cheer Championship is the official
8 state championship event for Indiana school cheerleading
9 teams;
10 Whereas, The Trojans' commitment to excellence was
11 evident in their performance competing against 12 other top
12 teams in the A/B Time-Out Only Division, taking home their
13 first ever IASP state championship;
14 Whereas, Members of the team include: Adriana Gorbea,
15 Alana Hernandez, Andie Kollar, Ashlyn Dolph, Bobby Spencer,
16 Breanne Pappas, Brendan Brady, Canon Surane, Carly Decker,
17 Cole Gilbert, Emma Gilbert, Ella Marganti, Ethan Wood, Gaby

19 Julia Depew, Kathryn Jasch, Kendall Tarnow, Kyliann Hill,
20 Levi Swart, Maddie Lyons, Mady Novak, Niko Monako, Olivia
21 Papp, and Sophia Ontiveros;
22 Whereas, The team is coached by: Tara Graf, Calla Dolph,
23 Cordie Armstrong, Emma Sieman, and Grant Gilbertson;

1 Whereas, The Trojans' competed in the UCA Regional
