35 (99.9%) pure; and
36 (2) coined, stamped, or imprinted with the weight and purity
37 of the precious metal.

39 fabricated into products that are:
40 (1) of uniform shape, size, design, content, weight, and purity;
41 and

7 (b) Specie may be recognized to pay:
