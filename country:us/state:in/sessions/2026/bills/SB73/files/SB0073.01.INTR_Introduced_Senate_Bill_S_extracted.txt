DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 35-31.5-2; IC 35-45-1.
Synopsis: Masks at public assemblies. Makes wearing a mask at a
public assembly a Class C misdemeanor, and increases the penalty to
a Class A misdemeanor for a second or subsequent offense. Provides
a defense under certain circumstances. Increases the penalty for rioting
and disorderly conduct to a Level 6 felony if the offense is committed
while wearing a mask.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Byrne
December 8, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Corrections and Criminal
Law.
