SENATE ENROLLED ACT No. 164
AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning agriculture and
animals.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
SECTION 1. IC 15-11-2-10 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
1, 2026]: Sec. 10. (a) The department shall conduct a study to
evaluate the feasibility of establishing community or urban
microfarm zones statewide.
(b) The study conducted under subsection (a) must include the
following:
(1) A summary of existing studies and literature regarding the
feasibility of establishing community or urban microfarm
zones statewide.
(2) Identification of zoning templates and best practices for
community or urban microfarm management.
(3) Opportunities for partnerships with the Purdue University
cooperative extension service, local health departments,
health care providers, and educational institutions.
(c) Not later than December 1, 2027, the department shall
submit a report to the general assembly, in an electronic format
under IC 5-14-6, with the following information:
(1) The findings regarding subsection (b)(1) through (b)(3).
(2) A specific finding as to:
(A) whether and how community or urban microfarm
