HOUSE BILL No. 1159
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 20-34-3.
Synopsis: Prekindergarten vision screening. Requires the governing
body of each school corporation to conduct vision testing for
prekindergarten children. Allows for the use of electronic eye charts.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Harris
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Education.
