DIGEST OF SB 2 (Updated February 11, 2026 2:53 pm - DI 106)
Citations Affected: IC 35-33.
Synopsis: Bail procedures. Provides that offenses other than murder
are not bailable if the state proves: (1) by a preponderance of the
evidence that the proof is evident or the presumption strong; and (2) by
clear and convincing evidence that the arrestee is dangerous and that
no conditions of release will reasonably protect the safety of other
persons or the community. Establishes procedures for a bail hearing.
Removes duplicative provisions concerning repeat violent arrestees.
Permits a court to retain cash bail for the payment of certain costs
without an indigency hearing if the defendant has executed an
agreement permitting the court to retain the cash bail for these
purposes.
Effective: January 1, 2027.

DIGEST OF SB 2 (Updated February 11, 2026 2:53 pm - DI 106)
Citations Affected: IC 35-33.
Synopsis: Bail procedures. Provides that offenses other than murder
are not bailable if the state proves: (1) by a preponderance of the
evidence that the proof is evident or the presumption strong; and (2) by
clear and convincing evidence that the arrestee is dangerous and that
no conditions of release will reasonably protect the safety of other
persons or the community. Establishes procedures for a bail hearing.
Removes duplicative provisions concerning repeat violent arrestees.
Permits a court to retain cash bail for the payment of certain costs
without an indigency hearing if the defendant has executed an
agreement permitting the court to retain the cash bail for these
purposes.
Effective: January 1, 2027.
