DIGEST OF SB 7 (Updated January 28, 2026 2:14 pm - DI 153)
Citations Affected: IC 14-39; IC 36-7.
Synopsis: Carbon sequestration. Provides that a storage operator may
not operate a carbon sequestration project that transports or stores
carbon dioxide outside the county where the carbon dioxide is
generated unless the project is approved by the appropriate county
legislative body or plan commission. Exempts certain projects from the
provisions of the bill. Makes conforming changes.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Niemeyer, Deery
December 8, 2025, read first time and referred to Committee on Utilities.
January 14, 2026, reassigned to Committee on Environmental Affairs pursuant to Rule
68(b).
January 27, 2026, reported favorably — Do Pass.
January 28, 2026, read second time, amended, ordered engrossed.
