SENATE BILL No. 12
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
elections.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 3-5-2.1-87.5 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA
2 CODE AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 87.5. "Ranked choice voting"
4 refers to a system of voting that:
5 (1) allows a voter to rank to each candidate on a ballot for a
6 particular office in an order of preference; and
7 (2) tabulates each ballot in multiple rounds following the
8 elimination of a candidate;
9 until a single candidate receives a majority of the votes cast.
10 SECTION 2. IC 3-5-4-16 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
11 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
12 1, 2026]: Sec. 16. The following apply to an election conducted
13 under this title:
14 (1) An election may not be determined by ranked choice
15 voting.
16 (2) A candidate may not be nominated for or elected to an
17 office by means of ranked choice voting.
