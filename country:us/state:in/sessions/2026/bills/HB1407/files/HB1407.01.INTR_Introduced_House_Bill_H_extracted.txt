HOUSE BILL No. 1407
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 4-32.3.
Synopsis: Contest based raffles. Allows qualified organizations to
conduct contest based raffles for charity gaming. Specifies the manner
in which contest based raffles must be conducted. Provides that a sport
horse competition may be used to determine the winners of a contest
based raffle.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Genda
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Public Policy.
