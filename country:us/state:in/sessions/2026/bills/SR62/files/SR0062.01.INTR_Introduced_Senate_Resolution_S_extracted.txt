SENATE RESOLUTION ____
MR. PRESIDENT:
I offer the following resolution and move its adoption:
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION honoring the work of the 425th
2 Personnel Service Company.
3 Whereas, The 425th Personnel Service Company (PSC) has
4 served as an administrative support unit within the United
5 States Army Reserve ever since its activation during the Cold
6 War Era. Throughout its history, the 425th PSC has maintained
7 its mission to provide personnel support and human resources
8 services to Army units;
9 Whereas, Renowned for its professionalism and dedication,
10 the 425th PSC has played an essential role in maintaining the
11 well-being and readiness of Army personnel. They have
12 supported operations from the Gulf War to peacekeeping in the
13 Balkans, and the Global War on Terror;
14 Whereas, The 425th PSC also supported the U.S. Army
15 Reserve unit based in Indianapolis, which was one of two units
16 mobilized out of Fort Benjamin Harrison and deployed to Saudi
17 Arabia over 35 years ago for Operation Desert Storm. The
18 425th PSC was awarded the Meritorious Unit Citation for their
19 service from late 1990 into early 1991, a pivotal moment for
20 that conflict;

5 Army Commendation Medals and achievement awards for
