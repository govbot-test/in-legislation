DIGEST OF HB 1137 (Updated January 14, 2026 11:01 am - DI 110)
Citations Affected: IC 20-26.
Synopsis: Foods and beverages on school property. Provides that a
public school that participates in a federally funded or assisted meal
program: (1) may not serve, sell, or allow a third party vendor to serve
or sell a food or beverage that contains certain ingredients on school
property on a school day; and (2) shall post any breakfast or lunch
menu and ingredients on the school's website. Establishes exceptions.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
McGuire, Smith H, Barrett, Culp
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Education.
January 15, 2026, amended, reported — Do Pass.

HOUSE BILL No. 1137
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
education.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 20-26-9-20 IS ADDED TO THE INDIANA CODE
2 AS A NEW SECTION TO READ AS FOLLOWS [EFFECTIVE JULY
3 1, 2026]: Sec. 20. (a) This section does not apply to the following:
4 (1) Vending machines on school property that are not
5 accessible by students.
6 (2) Events on school property that occur on the weekend.
7 (b) Beginning with the 2027-2028 school year and except as
8 provided under subsection (c), a public school, including a charter
9 school, that participates in a federally funded or assisted meal
10 program:
11 (1) shall post on the school's website the school's breakfast
12 and lunch menu, as applicable, with the list of ingredients for
13 each item on the menu; and
14 (2) may not serve, sell, or allow a third party vendor to serve
15 or sell a food or beverage on school property on a school day
16 that contains one (1) or more of the following ingredients:
17 (A) Potassium bromate (CAS 7758-01-02).
HB 1137—LS 6622/DI 110
2
1 (B) Propylparaben (CAS 94-13-3).
2 (C) Titanium dioxide (CAS 13463-67-7).
3 (D) Yellow dye 5 (CAS 1934-21-0).
4 (E) Yellow dye 6 (CAS 2783-94-0).
5 (F) Blue dye 1 (CAS 3844-45-9).
6 (G) Blue dye 2 (CAS 860-22-0).
7 (H) Green dye 3 (CAS 2353-45-9).
8 (I) Red dye 3 (CAS 16423-68-0).
9 (J) Red dye 40 (CAS 25956-17-6).
10 (K) Butylated hydroxytoluene (BHT) (CAS 128-37-0).
11 (L) Tert-butylhydroquinone (TBHQ) (CAS 1948-33-0).
12 (M) Azodicarbonamide (CAS 123-77-3).
13 (c) A school described in subsection (b) may sell or permit the
14 sale of a food or beverage that contains an ingredient listed in
15 subsection (b)(2) on school property as part of a fundraising event
16 if the sale of the food or beverage occurs at least thirty (30) minutes
17 after the end of the school day.
18 (d) This section does not prohibit the parent of a student from
19 providing a food or beverage that contains an ingredient listed in
20 subsection (b)(2) to the student on a school day.
