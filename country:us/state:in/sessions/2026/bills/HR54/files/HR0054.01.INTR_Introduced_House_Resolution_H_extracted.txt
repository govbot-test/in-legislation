1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION honoring the Floyd Central
3 team.
4 Whereas, The Floyd Central High School Navy Junior
5 Reserve Officers Training Corps (NJROTC) rifle team won the
6 NJROTC Precision Rifle National Championship for the second
7 consecutive year;
8 Whereas, The Floyd Central High School NJROTC rifle
9 team specializes in marksmanship and competes in shooting
10 competitions. Its members shoot with air rifles in three
11 positions: prone, kneeling, and standing, allowing them to
12 learn precision and accuracy while following strict safety
13 protocols;
14 Whereas, The Civilian Marksmanship Program, in

1 cooperation with the Missouri Marksmanship Education
2 Center, hosted the NJROTC National Championships at its new
3 state-of-the-art training facility in Columbia, Missouri, from
4 February 12 to February 14;
5 Whereas, Fifty-one sporter teams and twenty precision teams
6 gathered from across the country to compete for national
7 honors. Representing 22 states — and also a United States
8 Department of Defense school from Japan — the championship
9 field showcased the increasing strength and reach of scholastic
10 marksmanship;
11 Whereas, Competition was fierce from the first relay to the
12 final shot, but the Hoosier athletes from Floyd Central High
13 School rose to the occasion in commanding fashion;
14 Whereas, Cadets Megan Fondy, Logan Hall, Ysabelle
15 Martinez, Lacy Schilmiller, and Emma Velten make up the rifle
16 team that won the team competition with a score of 4,709,
17 which was 104 points higher than the second-place team;
18 Whereas, Lacy Schilmiller finished first in the nation in the
19 individual competition, Ysabelle Martinez finished second, and
20 Logan Hall finished sixth with scores of 464.5, 464.1, and
21 418.4, respectively;
22 Whereas, Master Gunnery Sergeant Lyn Akermon is the
23 NJROTC instructor for Floyd Central High School and trains
24 the members of the rifle team to be excellent marksmen;
25 Whereas, Competing on the rifle team requires cadets to be
26 extremely focused, patient, and disciplined, and they must excel
27 academically to participate; and
28 Whereas, Indiana shooting sports continue to demonstrate
29 not only competitive excellence but also the depth of talent
30 being developed within its scholastic programs, and the Floyd
31 Central High School NJROTC rifle team is a prime example:
32 Therefore,
33 Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the
34 General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
