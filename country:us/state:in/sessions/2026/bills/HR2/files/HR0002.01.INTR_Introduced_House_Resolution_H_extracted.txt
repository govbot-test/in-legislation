1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION celebrating the 70th anniversary
2 of the Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc., South Bend Chapter.
3 Whereas, Delta Sigma Theta, Incorporated, was founded
4 nationally on January 13, 1913, on the campus of Howard
5 University by 22 young women who had a calling to make a
6 difference, particularly in the Black community;
7 Whereas, Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc., is a private,
8 not-for-profit organization whose purpose is to provide
9 assistance and support through established programs in local
10 communities worldwide. The sorority currently has over 1,000
11 collegiate and alumnae chapters located in over 11 countries;
12 Whereas, The South Bend Alumnae Chapter of Delta Sigma
13 Theta Sorority, Inc., was the first Black Greek letter
14 organization in the Michiana area. It was initially chartered as
