DIGEST OF HB 1195 (Updated February 11, 2026 3:59 pm - DI 140)
Citations Affected: IC 20-26; IC 20-30; IC 22-4.1.
Synopsis: High school equivalency programs. Amends Indiana high
school equivalency diploma program eligibility requirements for
individuals under 18 years of age. Changes the expiration date of the
high school equivalency pilot program from June 30, 2026, to June 30,
2028. Makes conforming changes.
Effective: June 29, 2026; July 1, 2026.
