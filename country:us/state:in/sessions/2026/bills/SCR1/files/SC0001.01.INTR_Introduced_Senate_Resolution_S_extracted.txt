RESOLUTION No. _____
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION to allow the Senate and
2 the House of Representatives of the 124th Indiana General
3 Assembly to adjourn and recess separately throughout the
4 Second Regular Session for periods in excess of three (3)
5 consecutive days as the need, in the judgment of the President
6 Pro Tempore of the Senate and the Speaker of the House of
7 Representatives, respectively, may arise.
8 Whereas, The Indiana Senate and the House of
9 Representatives met for the purpose of organization of each
10 house and to conduct the public business of the people of the
11 State of Indiana;
12 Whereas, The Senate and the House of Representatives
13 shall operate on their own respective schedules, having been
14 separated from each other pursuant to IC 2-2.1-1-3;
15 Whereas, During the consideration of legislative business,
16 each house may, in the respective judgment of the President
17 Pro Tempore of the Senate and the Speaker of the House of
18 Representatives, deem it necessary to adjourn and recess for
19 periods in excess of three (3) consecutive days and to operate
20 on a schedule different from the other house;
21 Whereas, Article 4, Section 10 of the Constitution of the
22 State of Indiana states that neither house shall, without the
23 consent of the other, adjourn for more than three (3)
24 consecutive days;

9 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly

12 SECTION 1. That each house of the 124th Indiana General

17 of the Senate, by the President Pro Tempore of the Senate, or,
18 in the case of the House of Representatives, by the Speaker of
19 the House of Representatives.
20 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
