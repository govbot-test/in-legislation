SENATE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION No. 3
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION urging the Indiana
2 Department of Transportation to rename the portion of U.S. 12
3 between the borders of Michigan and Illinois the "Chief
4 Marshal James R. Kautz Memorial Highway".
5 Whereas, James Rodger Kautz was born on September 28,
6 1942;
7 Whereas, James was a veteran of the Vietnam War, having
8 served in the United States Army and been awarded three
9 Bronze Star Medals;
10 Whereas, Following his years of service in the Army, James
11 decided to serve the town of Long Beach, Indiana, as the Chief
12 Marshal beginning in 1977;
13 Whereas, On November 4, 1997, Chief Marshal Kautz was
14 on duty at the scene of a three-vehicle traffic accident on U.S.
15 12, east of State Road 212, when he was struck;
16 Whereas, Chief Marshal Kautz was taken to St. Anthony
17 Hospital before being airlifted to Christ Hospital in Oak Lawn,
18 Illinois, where he later passed on December 4, 1997;
19 Whereas, Chief Marshal Kautz is survived by his wife,
20 Cindy; and
21 Whereas, In honor of Chief Marshal Kautz's honorable
22 dedication to public service and his ultimate sacrifice, it is
23 fitting that the portion of U.S. 12 from the Michigan border to
2026 SC 3—RC 1512/DI SM
2
1 the Illinois border be renamed the "Chief Marshal James R.
2 Kautz Memorial Highway": Therefore,
3 Be it resolved by the Senate of the General Assembly
4 of the State of Indiana, the House of Representatives
5 concurring:
6 SECTION 1. That the Indiana General Assembly urges the
7 Indiana Department of Transportation to rename the portion of
8 U.S. 12 between the borders of Michigan and Illinois the "Chief
9 Marshal James R. Kautz Memorial Highway".
10 SECTION 2. The Secretary of the Senate is hereby directed
11 to transmit copies of this Resolution to the Commissioner of the
12 Indiana Department of Transportation.
