HOUSE BILL No. 1119
_____
DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 35-38-6.
Synopsis: Execution methods. Authorizes the death penalty to be
carried out by firing squad or nitrogen hypoxia: (1) if the commissioner
of the department of correction determines that use of a firing squad or
nitrogen hypoxia is advisable in light of availability considerations and
the resources of the department; or (2) upon request by the condemned
person. Establishes a procedure to authorize execution by firing squad
and by nitrogen hypoxia. Removes the requirement that an execution
be carried out: (1) at the Indiana state prison; and (2) before sunrise.
Makes conforming amendments and technical corrections.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Lucas, Prescott, Bartels, Ireland
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Courts and Criminal Code.
