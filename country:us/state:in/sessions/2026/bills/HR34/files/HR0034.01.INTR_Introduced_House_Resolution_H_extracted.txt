1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION honoring the Indiana Wesleyan
3 Moats.
4 Whereas, The Indiana Wesleyan University Wildcats have
5 achieved extraordinary success on the national stage,
6 demonstrating excellence in women's volleyball through
7 athletic competition and teamwork;
8 Whereas, Under the inspirational leadership of Head Coach
9 Candace Moats, who dedicated more than two decades to the
10 Indiana Wesleyan University program and more than forty
11 years to collegiate volleyball coaching, the Wildcats built a
12 tradition of sustained excellence, character, and faith-filled
13 leadership;
14 Whereas, In recent seasons, the Indiana Wesleyan University

2 congratulates the Indiana Wesleyan University women's
3 volleyball team for its remarkable achievements and

5 SECTION 2. That the Indiana House of Representatives
6 formally recognizes Head Coach Candace Moats for her
