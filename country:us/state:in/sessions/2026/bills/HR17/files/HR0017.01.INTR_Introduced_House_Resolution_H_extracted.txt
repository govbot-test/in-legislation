1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION recognizing the Indiana Trail
2 Riders Association.
3 Whereas, On May 16, 2025, an EF-2 tornado struck Brown
4 County State Park, causing significant damage to the
5 horsemen's campground, totaling millions of dollars;
6 Whereas, The tornado uprooted multiple trees and severed
7 the underground electrical wiring within the campground,
8 delaying plans to connect the area to Wi-Fi. Tragically, one
9 horse lost its life due to the storm;
10 Whereas, In response to the devastation, the Indiana Trail
11 Riders Association organized cleanup efforts, dedicating
12 thousands of volunteer hours and providing necessary
13 equipment. They also continued to assist following subsequent
14 storms that caused additional damage to the park; and

7 SECTION 1. That the Indiana House of Representatives
