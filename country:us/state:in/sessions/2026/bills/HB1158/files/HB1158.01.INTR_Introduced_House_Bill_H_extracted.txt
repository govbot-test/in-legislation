DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 20-30-5-6.
Synopsis: Positive personal outcomes. Includes instruction that
stresses the nature and importance of the positive personal and societal
outcomes associated with: (1) obtaining a high school diploma or high
school equivalency credential; (2) securing full-time employment; and
(3) waiting until marriage to begin having children; in existing good
citizenship instruction.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
DeVon
January 5, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Education.

HOUSE BILL No. 1158
A BILL FOR AN ACT to amend the Indiana Code concerning
education.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Indiana:
1 SECTION 1. IC 20-30-5-6, AS AMENDED BY P.L.246-2005,
2 SECTION 170, IS AMENDED TO READ AS FOLLOWS
3 [EFFECTIVE JULY 1, 2026]: Sec. 6. (a) This section applies only to
4 public schools.
5 (b) As used in this section, "good citizenship instruction" means
6 integrating instruction into the current curriculum that stresses the
7 nature and importance of the following:
8 (1) Being honest and truthful.
9 (2) Respecting authority.
10 (3) Respecting the property of others.
11 (4) Always doing the student's personal best.
12 (5) Not stealing.
13 (6) Possessing the skills (including methods of conflict resolution)
14 necessary to live peaceably in society and not resorting to
15 violence to settle disputes.
16 (7) Taking personal responsibility for obligations to family and
17 community.
2026 IN 1158—LS 6745/DI 143
2
1 (8) Taking personal responsibility for earning a livelihood.
2 (9) Treating others the way the student would want to be treated.
3 (10) Respecting the national flag, the Constitution of the United
4 States, and the Constitution of the State of Indiana.
5 (11) Respecting the student's parents and home.
6 (12) Respecting the student's self.
7 (13) Respecting the rights of others to have their own views and
8 religious beliefs.
9 (14) The positive personal and societal outcomes associated
10 with:
11 (A) obtaining a high school diploma or high school
12 equivalency credential;
13 (B) securing full-time employment; and
14 (C) waiting until marriage to begin having children.
15 (c) The department shall:
16 (1) identify; and
17 (2) make available;
18 models of conflict resolution instruction to school corporations. The
19 instruction may consist of a teacher education program that applies the
20 techniques to the students in the classroom to assist school corporations
21 in complying with this section.
