DIGEST OF HB 1359 (Updated February 16, 2026 12:08 pm - DI 149)
Citations Affected: IC 3-11; IC 3-11.5.
Synopsis: Scanning ballots. Specifies a process by which certain
counties may scan voted optical scan ballot cards during the early
voting period and on election day without using secrecy envelopes.
Makes conforming amendments. Reduces the period during which in
person absentee voting may occur at the office of the circuit court clerk
or a satellite office from 28 days to a 16 day period ending at noon on
the day before election day.
Effective: Upon passage.

DIGEST OF HB 1359 (Updated February 16, 2026 12:08 pm - DI 149)
Citations Affected: IC 3-11; IC 3-11.5.
Synopsis: Scanning ballots. Specifies a process by which certain
counties may scan voted optical scan ballot cards during the early
voting period and on election day without using secrecy envelopes.
Makes conforming amendments. Reduces the period during which in
person absentee voting may occur at the office of the circuit court clerk
or a satellite office from 28 days to a 16 day period ending at noon on
the day before election day.
Effective: Upon passage.
