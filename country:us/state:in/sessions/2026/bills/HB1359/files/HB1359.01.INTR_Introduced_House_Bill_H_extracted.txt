DIGEST OF INTRODUCED BILL
Citations Affected: IC 3-11; IC 3-11.5.
Synopsis: Scanning ballots. Specifies a process by which certain
counties may scan voted optical scan ballot cards during the early
voting period and on election day without using secrecy envelopes.
Makes conforming amendments.
Effective: July 1, 2026.
Wesco
January 8, 2026, read first time and referred to Committee on Elections and
Apportionment.
